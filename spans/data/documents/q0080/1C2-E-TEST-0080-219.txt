Mario Balotelli gatecrashed an Inter Milan press conference unveiling new manager Andrea Stramaccioni in an impromptu visit to his old club.
Balotelli makes an impromptu return to his old club
Stramaccioni took over following Claudio Ranieri’s departure on Monday. Balotelli unexpectedly walked into the conference to laughter from the reporters and shook Claudio’s hand who declared “What a surprise!”
