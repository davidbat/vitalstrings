Video: Mario Balotelli at Nike Barbershop
by Sir Lucas Leftfoot on 2012/05/21
Continuing Nike Football’s “My Time is Now” campaign leading into Euro 2012, check out the first offshoot ad featuring Man City’s Mario Balotelli in the Nike Barbershop. When asked what he wants, Mario replies “To be remembered,” foreshadowing some interesting hairstyles as well as what could be a big summer. Hit the jump for the full spot, which also features France’s Mamadou Sakho and Argentinian Javier Pastore, as well as a behind-the-scenes video.
