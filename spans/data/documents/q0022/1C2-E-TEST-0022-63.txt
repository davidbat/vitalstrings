Selena Marie Gomez [2] (born July 22, 1992) [1] is an American actress and singer . She is best known for playing Alex Russo on the Disney Channel series , Wizards of Waverly Place . She has starred in the television movies , Another Cinderella Story and Princess Protection Program . She is Catholic . [3] She was born in Grand Prairie , Texas , near Dallas .
