Selena Gomez, born July 22, 1992, is an actress best known for her role as Alex Russo in the Disney Channel Original series, 'Wizards of Waverly Place.' She appeared in several feature film movies and has released an album on Hollywood Records.
About Selena Gomez
Known for: Her portrayal of Alex Russo on the Disney Channel Original Series ‘Wizards of Waverly Place’
Selena Gomez got her acting start on the children's television program 'Barney & Friends.' She credits the show with teaching her how to act.
In 2004, she was discovered by the Walt Disney Corporation. Gomez appeared on 'The Suite Life of Zack and Cody,' 'Hannah Montana' and the 'Wizards of Waverly Place.'
In 2008, Gomez signed a record deal with Hollywood Records and contributed to the ‘Tinker Bell,' ‘Another Cinderella Story’ and ’Wizards of Waverly Place’ soundtracks. Her band, Selena Gomez & the Scene, released its debut studio album ‘Kiss & Tell’ on September 29, 2009.
