Big Break: The Wizards of Waverly Place
Biography
Selena Gomez was born on July 22, 1992 and grew up in Grand Prairie, Texas. She made her acting debut at age 7 when she played Gianna on “Barney and Friends.” It wasn’t until 2007 that she got her big break as one of the stars on Disney Channel series, “Wizards of Waverly Place.”
Selena Gomez is also a singer, she first performed songs for the soundtracks of several Disney movies she appeared in as well as the soundtrack for her hit show, “Wizards of Waverly Place.” In 2009, Selena broke out of the Disney music scene and formed her own band, “Selena Gomez and the Scene.”
The singer/actress also launched her own fashion line in 2010 called “Dream Out Loud by Selena Gomez.”
The young star recently is making headlines for dating fellow teen heartthrob, Justin Bieber. She was previously linked to another young star, Nick Jonas.
Did you know...
She was discovered in a Disney nationwide talent search when she was 10, and starred in several failed Disney Channel pilots before the success of The Wizards of Waverly Place.
She sings the theme song for The Wizards of Waverly Place.
Was once best friends with fellow Disney star – Demi Lovato whom she met while both working on Barney. The two have since shared a youtube show, “The Demi and Selena Show.”
Her mother named her after the famous Tejano singer, Selena.
In 2008, Selena was named a spokesperson for UNICEF’s Trick-of-Treat for UNICEF program. A year later she became the youngest UNICEF ambassador at age 17.
She is the owner of 5 rescue dogs.
Britney Spears has written three songs for Selena’s upcoming album.
She has received death threats from jealous Justin Bieber fans.
Selena had a recurring role on Hannah Montana and a guest spot on The Suite Life of Zach & Cody before she was ever on The Wizards of Waverly Place.
Selena’s theatrical film debut was in the movie, “Ramona and Beezus,” based on the children’s novel series by Beverly Cleary.
