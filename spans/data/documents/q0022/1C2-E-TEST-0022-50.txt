Katy Perry's Poppin' "Part Of Me" Premiere: A Complete Look!
Looking radiant in a red knee length red velvet dress and matching heels, Katy Perry made a glamorous entrance to the Hollywood premiere of her upcoming film “Katy Perry: Part of Me” on Tuesday evening (June 26).
The pop superstar joined up with co-directors Dan Cutforth and Jane Lipsitz as she made her way down the bright pink personalized carpet while later emerging from a giant tub of popcorn onstage as she delved into a surprise performance for the massive crowd gathered at the Grauman's Chinese Theatre venue.
