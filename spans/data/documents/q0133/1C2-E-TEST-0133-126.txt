Originally released: 2008
Category: SALSA/SON; SALSA
EditorsPick: This might turn out to be the best Christmas gift of the season: Emusica has produced a lavish 4-CD boxed set consisting of the label's iconic salsa tracks featuring all you might expect and more: Alegre All Stars, Johnny Pacheco, Ray Barretto, Celia Cruz, Hector Lavoe, Willie Colon, Eddie Palmieri, La Lupe, Cheo Feliciano, Ricardo Ray/Bobby Cruz, Harlow, Tito Puente, Roberto Roena and a ton of others. Disk four expands into more modern salsa, too, bringing in artists like La India, Jose Alberto, Frankie Ruiz, Puerto Rican Power, Andy Montanez and others. The set is complete with a beautifully printed and bound 40 page booklet, on heavy glossy stock paper, all within a hard-cover package that would be worthy of bookshelf display. 58 tracks of pure salsa scripture. Well done liners by Ernesto Lechner.
Very Highly Recommended. (BP)
