Home Depot Information
Industry: Home Improvement
City: Atlanta, GA
Founded in 1978, The Home Depot(R) is the world's largest home improvement specialty retailer and the second largest retailer in the United States, with fiscal 2001 sales of $53.6 billion.  The company employs more than 280,000 associates and has 1,459 stores in 49 states, Puerto Rico, seven Canadian provinces, and Mexico.  The company for the second year in a row was named sixth Most Admired Company in America by Fortune magazine, which has alsoranked it as America's Most Admired Specialty Retailer for eight consecutive years.
