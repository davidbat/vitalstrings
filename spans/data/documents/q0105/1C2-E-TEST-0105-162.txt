About Home Depot
Home Depot (NYSE: HD) is a major retail chain specializing in hardware, appliances, lumber, home, yard, and garden supplies. Home Depot is one ofr the world's largest home improvement retailers with more than 2,200 retail stores in the United States (including Puerto Rico, the U.S. Virgin Islands and Guam), Canada, Mexico and China. Home Depot is the fourth largest retailer in the U.S. and the fifth largest in the world.
