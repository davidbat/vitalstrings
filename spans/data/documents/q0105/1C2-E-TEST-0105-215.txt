Once again this year, two national home-improvement retailers -- Lowes & Home Depot are offering 10 percent discounts to active-duty personnel, reservists, retirees, honorably discharged veterans and their immediate families. Both companies have offered the holiday discounts to military personnel and family members for the last several years.
The Lowes discount runs from now until July 7 for purchases up to $5,000. The Home Depot program runs from July 3 to July 6, for purchases up to $2,000. Check with your local store for complete details.
