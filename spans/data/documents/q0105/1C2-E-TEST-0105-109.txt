Benefits
Home Depot offers associates the opportunity to choose plans and programs that meet individual and family needs through Your Total Value, The Home Depot's benefits and compensation programs.
Benefit plans are available to part-time hourly, full-time  hourly and salaried associates.  Eligible dependents, including same-sex domestic partners, may also be covered under most plans.
