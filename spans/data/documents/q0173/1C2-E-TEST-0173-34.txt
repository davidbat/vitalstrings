Description
This electronic stamper keeps track of the date and time for you, so when you're ready to stamp, it's up to the minute. It also prints 4-digit or 6-digit sequential numbers as part of your date/time message or independently, along with your choice of status messages (IN, OUT, PAID, FILE, REF, CFMD, RCVD). Additional features include an alarm clock, 200-year calendar and programmable security code. Keeps track of the date and time for you, so when you're ready to stamp, it's up to the minute!
