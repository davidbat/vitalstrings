ANTHONY SMITH - NFL's King of Tats
As the 2011/2012 NFL season recently came to a close and we crowned another victor, Urban Ink takes a moment to catch up with one of the "Champions of the Gridiron" who persevered through last year's lockout, stayed on top of his game, inked up, and delivered yet another memorable season for NFL fans around the world. What better champion to catch up with than two-time Super Bowl champion, Tennessee Titan safety, and ink enthusiast Anthony Smith?
