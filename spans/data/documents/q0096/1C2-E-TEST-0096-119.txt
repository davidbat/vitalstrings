General Rules
Alcohol Policy :
To purchase alcoholic beverages, a patron must present a valid MA driver's license, MA liquor ID, passport or military ID, which indicates the patron to be a least 21 years of age (MA ID cards, duplicate licenses, expired licenses, damaged licenses and driver's licenses, which do not have photos, will not be accepted). Out of state licenses are acceptable only if the patron is at least 25 years of age and another form of identification (e.g., credit card) will be required if the patron is between 25 and 30 years of age. For out of country patrons, only a valid Passport will be accepted as proof of identification.
No smoking is allowed in the building.

Miscellaneous
For Your Safety:  Guests attending TD Garden events are advised that bags, backpacks, luggage, coolers, parcels, briefcases and like articles will be strictly prohibited from the facility. All guests will be subject to search, at the Garden's discretion, of their person and/or possessions (including women's handbags of normal size which may be allowed entry after such search.) Patrons with prohibited articles will be turned away at the entrances and no storage or "check-in area" will be provided for such items. Enforcement will be without exceptions.
