 
Game instructions for Garden TD:
You are the owner of this garden. But the insects and pests are thirsty for your flowers in your garden. Your job is to protect your garden from insects and pests. Use the devise to eliminate them. Click on the devices and set them around the path to your garden. Click again to set them down. You can buy and sell the devices too. Don’t let the pests go into your garden.
