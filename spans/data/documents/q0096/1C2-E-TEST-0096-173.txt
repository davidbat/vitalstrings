There's little reason to doubt that sports team owners want their fans to have cellphone access. After all, the thousands of tweets, Facebook posts and photos that emerge from each game are basically free advertising. And when it comes to tweets, there are few places that generate so many in so short a time as hockey or basketball games, thanks to rabid fans. But if the signal strength is poor, those tweets won't happen.
