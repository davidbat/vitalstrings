Yawn
Yawning is when you stretch your mouth open wide.
No one really knows why we do it ( maybe lack of sleeping cause it ), but it seems to be contagious.
If you yawn in class, you’ll probably notice a few other people will start yawning, too. Even thinking about yawning can get you yawning.
Even though we yawn when we’re tired or bored, I hope you didn’t yawn while reading this !
