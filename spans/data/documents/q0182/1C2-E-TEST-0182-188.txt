FAQ: Why Do We Yawn When Working Out?
greatist.com — As soon as someone lets out a yawn, it spreads like wildfire across the room (and apparently just writing about yawning can set off the chain reaction as well). Traditionally, yawning has been an indicator of two things: boredom or fatigue. But yawning during exercise might actually be the brain’s version of an in-workout cool-down.
