Lettuce is a perennial favorite crop in the kitchen as dish or as salad.  With a proper cultural management practices, lettuce can be grown anytime of the year, especially in a greenhouse.  Lettuce is not selective to any soil type from clay loam to sandy loam soils, but ideally, it should be grown on fertile soil rich in organic matter.  If you want to plant lettuce during warm months, you must plant them in heavy soil, since heavy soil has high moisture retention and cooler.
