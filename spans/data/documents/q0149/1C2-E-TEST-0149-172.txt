At the encouragement of local Contractors, Stowe native, David Walker established Walker Construction in 1972 as a Concrete Subcontractor. In 1987, our reputable company expanded into sitework and excavation.
To this day, Walker Construction still provides topnotch services in both concrete and excavation in Lamoille County and Vermont.
From sidewalks, foundations, and slabs to septic systems, driveways, ponds and snowplowing; we are known for our reliability, accuracy and attention to detail.
We proudly donate to local and state charities and employ almost 50 community members.
If you are looking for an honest, highly regarded and experienced company with an outstanding reputation who still believe that customers come first, give us a call and find out first hand that your job will be Better by Walker.
