Shep Tube Concrete Forms
Shep Tube Concrete Forms are often referred to as Sonotube, successfully fills the need for a one-time fibre form that reduces the cost of pouring round columns, piers, piles, underpinning, and for encasing wood and steel piling.
Features & Benefits
Easily and quickly set in place
Lightweight forms are easy to handle, brace, and anchor
Can be readily cut and notched to fit beam tie-in, structural framing, and to form pilasters.
Produce clean, smooth columns at lower cost than other methods of forming
