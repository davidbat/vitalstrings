Fresh Fest , Keith Sweat , top 10 songs
The former member of LSG and host of The Keith Sweat Hotel will be coming to the Time Warner Cable Arena this Sunday July 1st along with Doug E. Fresh, K-Ci & Jo-Jo, SWV, and Guy. You can hear him nightly on WBLS FM as the voice for The Quiet Storm in New York.  Mr. Sweat has been recognized as a song writer and producer for popular artist and he is even accredited for the discovery of two popular R&B groups. He originally was the lead singer for a Harlem band called Jamilah before breaking away as a solo artist. Through the late 80s and early 90s he produced number 1 hits under Elektra Records during the era of New Jack Swing.
If you are an R&B fan, check out the top 10 hits by Keith Sweat and cruise down memory lane to recall which where you’re favorite and what you were doing when these songs hit the air waves on your radio. Nobody can croon like Keith Sweat…
“I Want Her”- Album: Make It Last Forever (1988) This was the first new jack swing song to reach #1 on the R&B charts. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7YJc80Xgubk
“Twisted”- Album: Keith Sweat (1996) Kut Klose, an R&B female group discovered by Sweat was featured on this song; it also peaked at number one on the Rhythmic Top 40, staying there for 14 consecutive weeks. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cP4yAnbtDZE
 “Make It Last Forever”- Album: Make It Last Forever (1988) The song was later covered by Chante Moore and Kenny Lattimore, then Mariah Carey. Keith’s version peaked at #2 on the Hot Black Singles chart. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sKgCLVl-bGY
 “Make You Sweat”- Album: I’ll Give All My Love to You (1990) Although this song spent only one week as #1 on the US R&B chart, it made it to #14 on the Billboard Hot 100. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dV0ea3IEo9o
 “I’ll Give All My Love to You”- Album: I’ll Give All My Love to You (1990) The song is featured as a live version on “Sweat Hotel: Live” with R&B singer Monica. It held the #7 spot on the Billboard Hot 100. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s8J_3zvH_7c
“Nobody”- Album: Keith Sweat (1996) The song was #3 on the US R&B charts and #3 on the Billboard Hot 100. It also featured a member from the Sweat discovered group Kut Klose. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FMar1ifzdmk
“Your Love”- Album: I’ll Give All My Love to You (1990) Held the #4 spot on the Hot R&B/Hip Hop Songs chart. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z3wks4Edevk
 “Keep it Comin”- Album: Keep It Comin (1991) The song was peaked #1 on the US Billboard Hot R&B Singles and #7 on the Japan (Tokyo Hot 100) http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4czRpi3CKTc
“Why Me Baby?”- Album: Keep It Comin (1991) Featured LL Cool J, and was written by James Todd, Teddy Riley and Keith Sweat. It was #2 on the US R&B chart. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S4F17ly1aeY
“Merry Go Round”-Album: I’ll Give All My Love to You (1990) This song held the #2 position on the Hot R&B/Hip Hop Songs Chart http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RgmGkLR4rzQ
