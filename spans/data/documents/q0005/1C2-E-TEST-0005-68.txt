R&B veteran Keith Sweat will release his 11th studio album, Open Invitation, on November 1, 2011 via eOne Music. The follow-up to last year’s ‘Ridin' Solo,’ will be lead by the first single "Make You Say Ooh," a track selected by his fans, which is reminiscent to Sweat's most popular hits.  Album: Open Invitation Label: eOne Music
