In her latest installment of “The Bitter & The Sweet,” Kandi is hanging with singer Keith Sweat and the topic of conversation is, “how do you determine who’s a real friend vs who is an acquaintance?” Keith breaks it down for Kandi as he tells her a real friend is someone who is there when you’re going through a rough time and sticks by you vs those who are only there for the good times.
