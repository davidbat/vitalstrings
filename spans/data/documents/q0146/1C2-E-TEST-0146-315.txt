Here you will find interesting facts about Thanksgiving Day History
Here are some facts about the first Thanksgiving and fun facts about Thanksgiving Day history
Thanksgiving Day is celebrated primarily in the United States and Canada.
In the United Sates, Thanksgiving Day is celebrated on the fourth Thursday in November.
In Canada, Thanksgiving Day is  celebrated on the second Monday in October.
Many believe the first Thanksgiving Day was held in the autumn of 1621 by the Pilgrims and the Wampanoag indians to celebrate the colony's first successful harvest.
Congress made Thanksgiving Day an official national holiday in the United States in 1941.
Thanksgiving Day is also known informally around the world as "Turkey Day".
