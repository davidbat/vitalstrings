Sir Winston Leonard Spencer Churchill, K. G
Born: 30 November 1874, Blenheim Palace, Woodstock, Oxfordshire, England
Died: 24 January 1965, Hyde Park Gate, London, England (aged 90)
Nationality: British
Education: Harrow School, Royal Military College, Sandhurst.
Interests: Painting, history, writing, bricklaying.
Occupation: Politician, orator, soldier in British Army, strategist, prolific author
Achievements: Prime Minister of United Kingdom 1940 to 1945 and 1951 to 1955
Politics: Conservative and Liberal
Spouse: He married Clementine Hozier on 12 September, 1908
Children: One son Randolph, four daughters Diana, Sarah, Marigold and Mary
Religion: Anglican
Comment: Churchill won the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1953 for his writings on British and world history. Known as the "British Bulldog".
