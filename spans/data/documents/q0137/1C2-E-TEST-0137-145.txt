Dental Assistant Schools in Connecticut
It might be one of the smallest states in the Union but Connecticut still has plenty of opportunities for someone wanting to become a dental assistant. With schools all across the tiny state it doesn't matter if you live in Hartford, Bridgeport or New Haven - there's bound to be somewhere for you to learn. Take advantage of online learning and learn at your own pace in the comfort of your own home!
As a Connecticut dental assistant, you will:
Work closely with, and under the supervision, of a dentist
Perform a variety of patient care, office and lab duties
Gather and sterilize instruments
Work with patients to take dental x-rays and process film
Some career figures for dental assistant professionals in Connecticut:
Average annual salary: $40,180
