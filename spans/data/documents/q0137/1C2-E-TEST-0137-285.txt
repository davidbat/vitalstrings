Your Safety On The Line
Developed by CL&P in collaboration with local fire, police and rescue personnel, "Your Safety on the Line" is designed to provide emergency workers and contractors with electrical safety best practices and guidelines to follow in the event of an accident involving electricity. It also provides life saving information for anyone who encounters an unsafe electrical hazard.
