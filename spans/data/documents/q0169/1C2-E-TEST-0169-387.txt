BCSLIB-EXT -- Award Winning Sparse-Matrix Package
BCSLIB-EXT is a Gordon Bell Award winning FORTRAN library of powerful mathematical software modules for solving large linear algebra problems. Initially developed by the Boeing Company, BCSLIB-EXT is now owned by Access Analytics International (AAI).
For more information about BCSLIB-EXT go to AAI web page at http://www.aanalytics.com .
