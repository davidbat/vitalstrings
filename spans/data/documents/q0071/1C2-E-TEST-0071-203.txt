The Players
Mauro Camoranesi
Mauro Camoranesi is a 33 year old Italian soccer player who was born on October 4, 1976. He plays as a mid fielder for club team Juventus and also the Italian National Team.
Christian Maggio
Christian Maggio  is a 28 year old Italian soccer player who was born on February 11, 1982. He plays as a defensive player for club team Napoli and also the Italian National Team.
Alberto Gilardino
Alberto Gilardino is a 28 year old Italian soccer player who was born on July 5, 1982. He plays forward position for club team Fiorentina and also the Italian National Team.
Domenico Criscito
Domenico Criscito is a 24 year old Italian soccer player who was born on December 30, 1986. He plays left back defender position for club team Genoa and also the Italian National Team.
Gianluigi Buffon
Gianluigi Buffon is a 32 year old Italian soccer player who was born on January 28, 1978. He plays goalie  for club team Juventus and  also the Italian National Team.
Leonardo Bonucci
Leonardo Bonucci is a 23 year old Italian soccer player who was born on May 1, 1987. He plays center back  defense position for club team Juventus and  also the Italian National Team.
Simone Pepe
Simone Pepe is a 27 year old Italian soccer player who was born on August 30, 1983. He plays in the winger position for club team  Juventus and  also the Italian National Team.
Gennaro Gattuso
Gennaro Gattuso is a 32  year old Italian soccer player who was born on January 9 , 1978. He plays as a defensive midfielder for club team AC Milian  and  also the Italian National Team.
Andrea Pirlo
Andrea Pirlo is a 31  year old Italian soccer player who was born on May 19 , 1979. He plays as a central midfielder for club team AC  Milian  and  also the Italian National Team. (He also is a free kick specialist.)
Fabio Cannavaro
Fabio Cannavaro  is a 37  year old Italian soccer player who was born on September  13 , 1973. He plays as a central back for club team Al-Ahli Dubai   and  also the Italian National Team. (Cannavaro is the proud captain of the Italian National Team)
Antonio Di Natale
Antonio Di Natale  is a 33  year old Italian soccer player who was born on October  13 , 1977. He plays as a striker for club team Udinese   and  also the Italian National Team.
