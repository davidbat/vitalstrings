Cannavaro wins Golden Ball, first defender to take award in 10 years
Italy captain Fabio Cannavaro won the Golden Ball award Monday, the first defender in 10 years to be named Europe's best player. Cannavaro, who played for Juventus last season but then moved to Real Madrid after helping Italy win the World Cup, got 173 points in France Football magazine's annual poll of 52 journalists from around the world.
