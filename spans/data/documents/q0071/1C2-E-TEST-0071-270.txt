Tuni 10 January, 2011 nai 4:00pm pawl akipan Kawlpi St. Mary biakinn mai aom BOC (Burma Oil Corporation) meikang in, mipi'n aphelhhang kiphelhzo paklo ahih manin ki patauman mahmah hi. Tua zumhuang sung aom Datsi puakna Motor pei 12 nei khatleh Mortor Cycle 2 kangtum hi. Ahih hangin Pasian huhnatawh 5:30pm pawlin kiphelhsiang zosam in, kiim leh pam alawh bangmah aomloh banah aliam abaizong kuamah kiom samlo cih ZYA makaite tungpan kizasawn hi.
Mei kipatna pen Telephone Bettary pan kipan hi dingin Kawlpi khangno khatin gen hi. Meiphelh dingin meiphelh Motorte a hun lap atun hangin, a sungah tui om lo ahih manin kiphelhzo paklo hi kici hi.
