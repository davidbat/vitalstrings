Barcelona
209 E. 6th St ( map )               (512) 236-0900
Barcelona is a huge part of Austin’s nightlife. Highly favored by Austin’s substantial hipster crowd, Barcelona hosts some of the greatest dance parties on a weekly basis in its trendy underground location. Bringing in performances by world-renown DJ’s while also serving as home to local talented spinners has placed Barcelona at the top of the 6th Street must-stop list.
