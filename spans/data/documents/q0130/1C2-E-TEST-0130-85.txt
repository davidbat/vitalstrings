Medusa Lounge
Medusa Lounge is open Wednesday-Saturday, 8pm-2am. The club ofers many red-cushioned lounge chairs and benches. A live DJ spins music from 8pm-10 pm, and then special guest DJs show up later in the night. The club has an upscale dress code policy:  Men cannot wear sandals, sports apparel or anything with offensive language on it; women cannot  wear any clothing with offensive language or images.
Food options include chicken strips, nachos, shrimp and salads. The lounge also is available for large private parties and alcoholic beverages are available for guests over 21.
