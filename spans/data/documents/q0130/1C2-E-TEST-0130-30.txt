Renowned for its 'beach by day' and 'party by night' reputation, Barcelona has a thriving night life with clubs open until 12pm the following day. Whether by the beach, in the center of town or a 15 minute cab ride away, there's a night club in Barcelona to suit everyone's taste. From hip hop, to electronic, top 40, to indie, to house and many more, Barcelona offers an incredible variety of clubs and parties from which to choose.
