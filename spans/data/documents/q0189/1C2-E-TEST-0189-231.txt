Unicoi County, TN
The Valley Beautiful
As its mountains tower over the Tennessee-North Carolina border, Unicoi County combines wonders of natural beauty with intriguing stories of history and culture. Here, elegant small towns bustle with sidewalk shops and eateries, locals recount fascinating stories of the past and future, and visitors stand in astonishment of nature's true marvels.
We invite you to enjoy our picturesque scenery, embrace our local culture, and experience the spirit-lifting wonder that only exists in "The Valley Beautiful."
