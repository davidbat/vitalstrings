VISIT AAMODT’S APPLE FARM!
Aamodts’ Apple Farm is a favorite family destination for fresh Minnesota apples and fall fun. Our sweet and juicy Honeycrisp apples are a seasonal treat! Purchase your favorite apple varieties inside our nostalgic apple barn. Come out and enjoy the apple orchard’s festive farm atmosphere near scenic Stillwater, Minnesota, just a short drive from Minneapolis/St. Paul.
