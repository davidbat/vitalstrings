Apple Spice Junction offers box lunch delivery, corporate catering and deli dining.
Sandwich selections feature freshly-baked breads, fine meats and cheeses and crisp vegetables. Whether you need a caterer for a business event or family party, or just want to stop in for a sandwich and a bowl of soup, Apple Spice Junction means "good food when you need it."
