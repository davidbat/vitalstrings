The 2012 williamhill.com Greyhound Derby – the world’s greatest greyhound race – will this year begin on Thursday 26th April at Wimbledon Stadium, London.
With a history stretching back to 1927, the williamhill.com Greyhound Derby is the most prestigious race on the British greyhound racing calendar, and arguably the biggest greyhound competition in the world.
Over 200 dogs from across Europe are expected to be entered into the Derby, which spans over four weeks, culminating with the Final on Saturday 26th May 2012.
