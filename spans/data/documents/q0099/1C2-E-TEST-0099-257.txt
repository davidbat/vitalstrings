Kingsmeadow, home to AFC Wimbledon and Kingstonian FC, is Kingston's premier conference and party venue. We can offer a wide range of versatile function rooms accommodating from 30 to 300 guests.
With car parking for over 140 cars, and within easy reach of Kingston and Wimbledon through excellent public transport links, Kingsmeadow is the ideal venue to host your wedding reception, party or conference.
