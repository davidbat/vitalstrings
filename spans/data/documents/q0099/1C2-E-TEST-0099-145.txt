Tournament Recap
June 21
In the first round of men's singles, top seeded Roger Federer lost the first two sets to Alejandro Falla and needed a fourth set tie breaking victory to avoid defeat. He won the fifth set 6-0 to advance to the second round.  Number five seed Andy Roddick advanced with a straight set victory over Rajeev Ram 6-3, 6-2, 6-2.  There were a few upsets on the first day.  Michal Przysiezny defeated number 17 seed Ivan Ljubicic  7-5, 7-6, 6-3. Peter Luczak defeated number 30 seed Tommy Robredo 2-6, 7-6, 6-2, 7-5.  Florian Mayer defeated 11th seeded Marin Cilic 6-2, 6-4, 7-6 and number 20 seed Stanislas Wawrinka lost in five sets to Denis Istomin, 7-6, 1-6, 6-2, 6-7, 3-6.
