Greyhound Derby
Greyhound Derbys in the United Kingdom
There are three derby races that are currently held in the United Kingdom, these races attract the television cameras and a big prize purse that make them the must win events for owners and trainers.
The most widely known and highly attended of these races is the world famous English Greyhound Derby which is held annually at Wimbledon Stadium . 
There is also the Scottish Derby which again is held annually, this comes from Shawfield Stadium in Glasgow and the event takes place in April.
Finally there is the Northern Irish greyhound derby which takes place in June each year and comes from Drumbo Park Stadium in Lisburn. Drumbo is one of only two greyhound stadiums still operating in Northern Ireland.
