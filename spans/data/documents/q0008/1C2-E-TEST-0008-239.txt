Lil Wayne is an American rapper , songwriter , and record producer . He was born Dwayne Michael Carter, Jr. on September 27 , 1982 , in New Orleans Louisiana .
http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1211443/bio
He began rapping at a young age, and signed a major recording contract with Cash Money Records at the age of 12. He began his career as a member of the rap group, the Hot Boyz . Once the group disbanded, Wayne continued to record as a solo artist. He released his first solo album, The Block is Hot , in 1999 . Over the span of his 10 year solo career, Lil Wayne, who also goes by the stage names Lil' Weezy , Weezy F. Baby , and Young Carter , has released a total of 7 studio albums and 11 mixtapes . In addition to his work in music, Wayne has also made a number of film and television appearances.

His career has been marred by a number of legal troubles, including arrests on drug and weapons charges.  He is currently serving a one year prison sentence for weapons possession.
