Compound Interest Amount Calculator
This calculator requires the use of Javascript enabled and capable browsers. This calculator is designed to calculate the total interest of a compound interest financial contract; the formulae for those calculations are below. This calculator supports the first 3 of them. Enter the values in the respective fields and click Calculate. If you wish to enter a fraction of a year, you must have a leading number. For instance, 1.5 years is valid and 0.5 years is also. However, .5 years is not.
Formulae for the various compound interest calculations...
Interest = Principal(1+(Rate/100))n Annual
