Compound Interest is also similar to simple interest but the interest earned will be added to the principal after a certain unit of time, from that moment on, the interest will be paid both on the principal and on the interest that has been added to it. We can say it simply in other words that the interest earns itself interest. The compounding period and the interest rate are the major components of compound interest calculation. The certain period after which an interest earned during that period added to the principal is called as Compounding Period. Interest Rate is the rate on which the money is borrowed or saved. In Compound Interest the Principal amount changes after every compounding period such as the amount after the first unit of time becomes the principal for the second unit, the amount after the second unit of time becomes the principal for third time and so on. After that certain period, the difference between the amount and the money borrowed is the Compound Interest.
