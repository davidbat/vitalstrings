Book Description
Publication Date: May 5, 1992
Realtors, bankers, and investors seeking to evaluate financial opportunities and make better investment decisions faster can consult this tables book for solving virtually all interest and value problems related to investing, loans, annuities, and accumulating wealth. This new edition includes daily compounding - in addition to monthly, quarterly, semiannual and annual compounding - as well as concise explanations, revised practice problems, and new examples that help readers avoid common errors.
