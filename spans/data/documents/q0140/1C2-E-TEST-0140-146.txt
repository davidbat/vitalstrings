Welcome to Lincoln Beach Oregon (population 2078).
Lincoln Beach is a small community on the Oregon Coast.
Recreational activities include, fishing, surfing, beachcombing, whale watching and more. Lincoln Beach is a great place to watch the storms roll in from the Pacific during winter. During the summer, it's a great place pass the time soaking up the sun or strolling along the miles of open beach.
One of the best areas on the coast for checking out tide pools and birdwatching is Fogarty Creek State Park, just a few miles down the road. Also nearby is the D River, the world's shortest river.
Lincoln Beach is located just 10 miles south of Lincoln City on the Oregon Coast Highway.
