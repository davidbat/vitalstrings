This Oregon Coast beach house vacation rental is the ideal place for a luxurious quick weekend getaway, or a once in a lifetime family vacation.
 
North Coast Lodge, is the perfect Oregon Coast Beach house vacation rental.  The lodge is located in Gearheart, Oregon, one of the most romantic settings on the Oregon coast. Just 15 minutes from Cannon Beach, less than 3 miles from Seaside Oregon it is the perfect location for your family getaway.
 
With 3 bedrooms and 2 baths on the main floor this vacation rental is easily accessible for the whole family. The large Master suite upstairs is a quiet retreat, where you can go to sleep with the sound of the waves, and wake up to your own private view of the ocean.
