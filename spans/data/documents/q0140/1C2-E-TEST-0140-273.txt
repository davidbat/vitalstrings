Entertainment - Gold Beach, Oregon
Gold Beach, Oregon is about 50 miles north of the California border.  The Rogue River pours into the Pacific Ocean from deep inland. Galice is where a lottery takes place to ride the whitewater rapids that are Class 6 and end at Gold Beach. You can drive from Gold Beach to Galice on the most beautiful drive and end and have lunch at Galice Resort, outside patio is fantastic. This is where the forest, river and ocean meet.
