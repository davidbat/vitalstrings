meet the Manager
Ashley-Marie Lamb
Having grown up on a farm in south Georgia, Ashley has always been an animal lover. She has devoted her life and career to the care and management of animals. With seven plus years of veterinary experience and a B.S. in Agriculture with a specialization in Animal Science, she has worked with a wide variety of animals from live stock to loyal pets. Ashley has worked in Pet Paradise locations around Northern Florida before becoming the manager at the Atlanta Airport. Pictured here with Ashley are her two dogs Luna (black lab) and Benelli (choc lab).
