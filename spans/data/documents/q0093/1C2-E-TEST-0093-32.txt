Make yourself at Home in Atlanta
The Hilton Garden Inn Atlanta Airport/Millenium Center hotel is only a mile south of Hartsfield-Jackson International airport, and is convenient to Atlanta’s most popular attractions. Find terrific shopping at the nearby Camp Creek Market Place. Access to Georgia I-75/85 and Georgia I-285 provides easy travel to attractions and corporate offices.
Enjoy complimentary high-speed internet access, and attentive service whenever you stay. Each morning, enjoy a tasty cooked-to-order breakfast from the Great American Grill® restaurant. Pick up snacks and beverages from the 24-hour Pavilion Pantry® and dine from the comfort of your guest room. Work out in the fully-equipped fitness center and watch the 37-inch flat panel TV as you take advantage of cardio machines and weights.
About the Hotel
Great Rooms
Find everything you need for a productive and relaxing stay in a comfortable guest room at this Atlanta airport hotel. Sleep well on the patented Garden Sleep System® bed, when you choose a king room, with an adjustable mattress and high-thread count linens. Watch the HD TV, call on the two dual-line phones and surf the net with the complimentary wired/wireless high-speed internet access. Work at the large work-desk, with an ergonomic Mirra chair by Herman Miller and use the complimentary remote printing to the business center. Prepare and store snacks with in-room refrigerator and microwave oven.
