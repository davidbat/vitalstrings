Restaurants Near Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport
The capital of Georgia, Atlanta is one of the country's foremost entertainment, transportation and business hubs. Tourists are drawn to the city by its cultural attractions, such as the Fox Theatre, the Atlanta Symphony Orchestra and the Center for Puppetry Arts. Though your stay in the city might be a layover of a few hours, a number of Atlanta's best restaurants are within a ten minute drive of Atlanta's Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport.
