ATL Atlanta Airport Hotels Park Sleep Fly Packages
There are also Atlanta Airport Hotels that offer Park Sleep and Fly Packages.  Park Sleep and Fly is an alternative to Airport Long  Term Parking. The traveler checks  into the Atlanta Airport Hotel the day before their flight and goes to the airport the next day  on the airport hotel parking shuttle.  When they return they call for the hotel  airport parking shuttle and  are brought back to the hotel where they pick up their car. 
Atlanta Airport Hotels that offer Park Sleep Fly          Packages include Comfort Suites - Morrow Atlanta Airport, The Clarion Hotel Atlanta       South, Super 8 Airport West and Best Western Atlanta Airport East.  Information on these hotels and other Atlanta Airport Hotels that offer Park Sleep     and Fly packaged can be found on our sister site www.SearchParkSleepFly.com .  Go to the Atlanta Airport Hotels Park Sleep Fly page.

ATL Atlanta Airport Information
Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL) is     operates by Department of Aviation for the City of Atlanta.  The     airport consists of a terminal building and 6 concourses labeled T,     A, B, C, D, and E - international concourse. Over 25 Domestic and     International Airlines service the Atlanta Airport.  There is complete list of airlines on the Passenger Airlines at Hartsfield-Jackson (ATL) page.  To view Terminal Maps for ATL Atlanta Airport go to the Terminal Layout page.  Unfortunately the airline page and terminal maps do not include the gate locations for airlines, but you can get this     information by    going to the Atlanta          Airport Flight Information Overview page and selecting you airline from the Arrivals or Departures drop down menu.
