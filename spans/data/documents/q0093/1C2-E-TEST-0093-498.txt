 
Finest Steakhouse...
Experience the “Finest Steakhouse South of the City.” Bentleys Steakhouse offers casual sophisticated dining in a warm contemporary setting, serving the finest USDA Steaks, Silverton Lamb, South Australian Lobster and signature side dishes. The “Wine Spectator” award winning wine list features more than 150 world-class wines and will surely please the most discerning palate. Imported French-pressed coffee presentations, delectable desserts and after dinner cordials complete this incredible dining experience.
Intimate, cozy dining chambers, individually appointed with gallery art, provide the ideal setting for business or romance. Rich, dark wood flooring is complimented by a custom area rug with bold, vibrant colors. From the moment you enter, you will feel the elegant environment warmed with southern hospitality, superior service and exquisite food.
