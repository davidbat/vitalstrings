First and current female Speaker of the House
Biography
Nancy Patricia D'Alesandro Pelosi (born March 26, 1940) is an American politician who is the current Minority Leader of the United States House of Representatives . Pelosi served as the 60th Speaker of the United States House of Representatives from January 4, 2007, to January 3, 2011. She was the first woman to hold the office and the highest-ranking female politician in American history.
