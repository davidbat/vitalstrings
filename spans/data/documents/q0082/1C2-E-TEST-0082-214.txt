Sunday, The 24th of  June 2012
Timo Glock will not race in the European Grand Prix today.  The German was unable to qualify yesterday after becoming unwell after the third practise session.  Despite not taking part in qualifying the stewards would have allowed him to start from the back of the grid as his practise times showed that he would have been in 107%.  On Sunday morning he underwent further tests and the Marussia team confirmed that he has an intestinal infection and after medical advise he will not race..  ... full article
