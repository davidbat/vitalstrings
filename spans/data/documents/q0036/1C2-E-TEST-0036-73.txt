Charlize Theron on "Sleepwalking" and Producing
By now, audiences are used to seeing the gorgeous Charlize Theron glam down for roles. Theron says that it’s not about the transformation so much for her as it is getting into the meat of a character by changing her style to match the role. “It’s about finding a story that you emotionally tap into and then servicing that story as the best as you possibly can,” explained Theron.
