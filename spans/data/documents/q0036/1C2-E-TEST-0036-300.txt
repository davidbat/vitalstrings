About Charlize Theron
Charlize Theron, born on August 7, 1975 in Benoni, South Africa, is an actress who is best known for her roles as Jill Young in 'Mighty Joe Young,' Candy Kendall in 'The Cider House Rules,' Stella Bridger in 'The Italian Job,' Aileen Wuornos in 'Monster,' Britt Ekland in 'The Life and Death of Peter Sellers,' Josey Aimes in 'North Country,' the title character in 'Æon Flux,' and Mavis Gary in 'Young Adult.'

About Ryan Reynolds
Ryan Reynolds is a Canadian actor best known for starring the romantic comedies 'The Proposal' and 'Just Friends'. He is also the titular star of 'The Green Lantern' (2011).
