More About Covergirls.co.za.
Covergirls.co.za is unlike any other modelling website. In fact, our aim is to provide our models and members with a complete new online experience. Experienced models and new comers to the modelling world are all welcome to apply and join our website. As a Covergirl.co.za model, you will receive free exposure in our monthly magazine publication and even stand a chance to win our exclusive “Cover Girl of the Month” award.
If you do not have what it takes to be a model, but want to be involved in the modelling and fashion industry, you can join as a normal member. Normal members will not be models, but will have the opportunity to vote for their favourite models on the website, and help us determine who will be the “Cover Girl of the Month”. Normal members are Covergirls.co.za judges.
