Charlize Theron
London, March 31 - Oscar winner Charlize Theron is set to be honoured for her decade of film achievements at an upcoming Las Vegas convention of theatre owners.
The actress shot to fame in the 1990s by becoming the first South African to win an Oscar in a major acting category.
Now she's set to be singled out for her career when she receives the Distinguished Decade of Achievement in Film Award at CinemaCon April 26, reorts contactmusic. com.
