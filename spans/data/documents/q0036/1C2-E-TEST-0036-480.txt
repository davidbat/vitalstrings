Fameball Profile of Charlize Theron http://www.fameball.com/Star/Charlize_Theron Charlize Theron (born August 7, 1975) is a South African American actress, film producer, and former fashion model. She rose to fame in the late 1990s following her roles in <i>2 Days in the Valley</i>, <i>Mighty Joe Young</i>, <i>The Devil's Advocate</i>, and <i>The Cider House Rules</i>. She received critical acclaim and an Academy Award for her portrayal of serial killer Aileen Wuornos in the film <i>Monster</i>, for which she became the first African to win an Academy Award in a major acting category. She received another Academy Award nomination for her performance in <i>North Country</i>.<br><br>She was named <i>Esquire's</i> Sexiest Woman Alive in 2007. In late 2008 she was asked to be a UN Messenger of Peace by the UN Secretary General Ban Ki-Moon.
