Key Stock Data:
Money Flow measures the relative buying and selling pressure on a stock; it represents the dollar value of composite uptick trades minus the dollar value of downtick trades. Values are updated intraday during market hours and are delayed at least 15 minutes. Source: WSJ Market Data Group.
Short interest data updated twice monthly. Source: WSJ Market Data Group
Dividend Yield and P/E Ratio updated intraday during market hours and are delayed at least 15 minutes. Dividend amount and share related data updated quarterly. Market Cap reflects shares outstanding multiplied by stock price; both values are as of the close of trading two days prior until roughly 6 p.m. ET; by 6 p.m. ET both values are as of the prior day's close. For companies with multiple common share classes, market capitalization includes both classes.
U.S. stock indexes are updated in real-time. Source: Dow Jones
