Dominos Pizza manager accused of stealing $30,000
FRIDAY, MAY 11, 2012
A A
PAMELIA  A manager for Dominos Pizza has been accused of stealing more than $30,000 from the store she worked at in Seaway Plaza, Route 11.
State police in Watertown arrested Kathy E. Putnam, 47, 691 Mill St.,on one count of third-degree grand larceny, a Class D felony.
Investigators said that $30,600 was discovered stolen between July 1, 2011, and March 31 of this year after accountants reconciled the stores books.
She was arraigned in the Pamelia Town Court by Town Justice Richard Baker and remanded to the Metro-Jefferson Public safety Building on $5,000 bail, pending Grand Jury action.
