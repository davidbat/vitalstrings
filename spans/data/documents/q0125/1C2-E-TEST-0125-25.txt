Description of volunteer effort:
This wonderful event will start at San Francisco City Hall for opening ceremonies in recognition of Starbucks 20th Anniversary of the 1st Starbucks store in San Francisco. Like last years incredible Ocean Beach event we are partnering with SF DPW Community Clean Team to beautify San Francisco. To celebrate volunteers will divide into 11 teams and conquer 11 different community service projects. The volunteer work will include painting, cleaning, weeding, pruning and planting.
When: 04/28/12 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Where: 1 Dr. Carlton B. Goodlett Place, San Francisco, CA 94102
Activity: Environment
