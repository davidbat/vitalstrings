7956 Ewok Attack is a LEGO Star Wars set that was released in the summer of 2011 . It contains two Ewoks ( Tokkat & Logray ) and a Scout Trooper . The Ewoks minifigures are new minifigures. The Scout Trooper is an old figure, which was previously included in other sets. This was the second set to be named Ewok Attack, after 7139 , which was released in 2002 . It also features a Speeder bike . The "tree" fort thing that came in the set was also
rigged with many traps.
