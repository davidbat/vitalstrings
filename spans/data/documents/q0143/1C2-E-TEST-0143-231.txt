Small: As small creatures, Ewoks gain a +1 size bonus to their Defense and attack rolls, and a +4 size bonus on Hide checks, but they are forced to use smaller weapons than Humans thus also reducing their lifting and carrying limits to three-quarters of those of Medium-size characters.
Ewok base speed is 6 meters.
Primitave: An Ewok receives the bonus feat Weapon Group Proficiency (primitive weapons) and Weapon Group Proficiency (simple weapons) at first level instead of the normal class feats available.
Ewoks receive the Alertness feat as a bonus at first level.
Automatic Languages: Speak Ewokese.
