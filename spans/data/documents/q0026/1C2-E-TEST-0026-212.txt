Alias Description
Alias was an action/drama series from ABC that starred Jennifer Garner, Victor Garber, Michael Vartan, Ron Rifkin, Bradley Cooper, Carl Lumbly, and many more over its five seasons. The series revolved around double agent Sydney Bristow (Jennifer Garner), who works as an operative for a criminal organization named SD-6 but also as a spy for the CIA. The CIA deploys Sydney to find and recover artifacts created by Milo Rambaldi, a Renaissance inventor whose works were both incredibly advanced and tied to prophecy, before they can fall into the hands of SD-6 or other rogue organizations. Jack Bristow (Victor Garber) is Sydney’s father, and is also a deep-cover double agent for the CIA. Arvin Sloane (Ron Rifkin) is the head of SD-6 and is obsessed with the works of Rambaldi, which he will stop at nothing to obtain. Sloane is the main antagonist of the series. Michael Vaughn (Michael Vartan) is Sydney’s CIA parter and Will Tippin (Bradley Cooper) is her friend and a reporter. A plethora of other talented cast members starred in the series throughout its run. Alias, which was created by notorious writer and producer J.J. Abrams, was nominated for several Emmy Awards and won a Golden Globe for Jennifer Garner as a lead actress. MORE
