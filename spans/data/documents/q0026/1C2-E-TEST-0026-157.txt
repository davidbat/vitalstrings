Biography
Actress Jennifer Garner was born in Houston, Texas on the 17th of April 1972. She is best known as Sydney Bristow from the television series Alias. Before Alias she starred in numerous T.V. shows and movies including Dude, Where's My Car? with Ashton Kutcher. She has done a video for the CIA that is available on the CIA Website. In 2001 Jennifer Garner starred with Kate Beckinsale in Pearl Harbor. She will soon star in the romatic comedy Ghosts of Girlfriends Past.
