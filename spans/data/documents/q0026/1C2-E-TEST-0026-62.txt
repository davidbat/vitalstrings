DOWNLOAD JENNIFER GARNER IN ALIAS WALLPAPER 1 (size: 800 x 600 pixels)
Instruction:
For PC Users:
Position your cursor over the image and click your RIGHT mouse button. A menu box should pop up. From the menu, select SET AS WALLPAPER or SET AS BACKGROUND. This will automatically set the image as your wallpaper.
For Mac Users:
Click and hold on the image, then save it to your hard drive. In your Desktop Pictures control panel, click SELECT PICTURE (if you already have a picture, click REMOVE PICTURE first, then SELECT PICTURE) and choose the image. Click SET DESKTOP and close the control panel.
