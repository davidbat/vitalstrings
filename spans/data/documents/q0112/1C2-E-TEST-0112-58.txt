About Us
Boston Duck Tours take you on a tour of Boston unlike any other one! See the exciting city of Boston by land, and by water, all while riding in the same vehicle. The adventure begins as you step onto the "DUCK," a World War II style amphibious landing vehicle where you are greeted by a conDUCKtor who narrates the tour. You will cruise by places that make Boston famous for its freedom, such as golden dome State House. From there, you will continue past Bunker Hill and the TD Banknorth Garden, Boston Common and Copley square to the Big Dig, Government Center to fashionable Newbury Street, Quincy Market, and the Prudential Center, among many other attractions.
As for your cruise through the streets and waterways of Boston, you will receive small tidbits and little known facts about this unique and wonderful city.
Features
Hop aboard Boston Duck Tours and enjoy a fully narrated, historic tour of Boston in a DUCK, a W.W.II style amphibious landing craft that travels on land and water.  You'll cruise by all the places that make Boston the birthplace of freedom and a city of firsts. Just when you think you've seen it all, your ConDUCKtor drives the DUCK right into the Charles River for a breathtaking view of the Boston and Cambridge skylines.
