Welcome to Segway of Boston
Segway of Boston is YOUR Authorized Segway Tour Provider. Offering Guided Tours of Cambridge and Boston, we pride ourselves on giving you a truly unique experience.
The demands of our lifestyles and our environment have forced us to evolve both economically and eco-consciously. There is no better way to satisfy those needs than owning a Segway Personal Transporter.
The Museum of Science has teamed up with Segway® of Boston to offer the Museum of Science Segway® Experience, educational tours through Cambridge using innovative personal transportation vehicles. The tour will also emphasize the rich history, key landmarks, local architecture, wildlife, and diverse habitats of Cambridge, Boston, and surrounding areas.
