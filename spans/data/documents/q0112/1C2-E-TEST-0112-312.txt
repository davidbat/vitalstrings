Singapore Duck Tours is the first and only amphibious themed attraction in Asia!     Duck Tours offers a combined City and Harbour Tour without the hassle of switching craft.
Be prepared and hold onto your seat in this genuine Vietnam War machine as it waddles     away for a splashing good time!
Duck Route: The Duck will embark on its journey from Suntec City and go by places such as     Victoria Theatre, Supreme Court and the War Memorial Park.
It will then proceed down to Marina Bay where you will be able to see The Esplanade and the Merlion statue.     You will then return to Suntec City where you may alight.
