A Budget Hotel in Manhattan
just 3 blocks from Central Park
on the Upper West Side
The newly renovated Belnord welcomes the budget-minded traveler with its warm, friendly service and discount rates. Located in the fashionable Upper West Side of Manhattan, the Belnord is placed on a quiet residential street. Situated near the 86th street subway station on Broadway and three blocks from Central Park, the Belnord is a quaint boutique hotel designed for the discount traveler. The hotel is minutes away from fine dining, shopping, entertainment venues and local NYC tourist attractions. The Belnord is perfect for the budget minded traveler seeking comfortable lodging accommodations , excellent location and good value.
