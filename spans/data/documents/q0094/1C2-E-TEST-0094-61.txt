 Howard Johnson Express Inn - Queens
 
6509 Queens Blvd - Woodside, NY, United States 11377
 
 
Howard Johnson Express Inn – Queens is located five miles from the LaGuardia Airport as well as from Manhattan. Going to the city of New York is easy since there is a bus stop and a subway station near the hotel. Guests can enjoy amenities like wireless Internet in lobby as well as in rooms, televisions, in-room safes, etc
