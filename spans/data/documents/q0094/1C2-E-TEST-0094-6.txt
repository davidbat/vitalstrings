The Latham Hotel
Just off 5th Avenue in the heart of Manhattan, The Latham Hotel offers no-frill accommodations for the budget-minded traveler. Guests can choose between one double bed and two twin beds, and between shared or private bathrooms. All guest rooms include a telephone, air conditioning and color television with HBO. High-speed wireless Internet access is available for an additional fee. You don't need much in your room because there is plenty to do outside--the Empire State Building is within walking distance and within one mile you'll find Radio City Music Hall, Times Square and Madison Square Garden. As of October 2010, room rates start at $99 for a double bed with shared bath and $131 for a double bed with private bath.
