The Solid Waste Management Department provides services such as residential and commercial trash collection and disposal, large item disposal, recycling, graffiti removal, convenience centers and neighborhood cleanup support.
Our other services include:
Operating the City's landfills in compliance with State and Federal regulations
Ensuring environmental safety at old landfills
Managing and operating a recycling processing facility.
Educating the public about recycling and responsible waste disposal.
Integrated Waste Management
The Solid Waste Management Department initiated an Integrated Waste Management Plan (IWMP) to assist the Department in setting short-term and long-term goals for a sustainable, comprehensive solid waste program.
