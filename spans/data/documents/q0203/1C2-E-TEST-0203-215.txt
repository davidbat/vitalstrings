Resource Equipment Company specializing in balers and compactors since 1990.
As Waste Removal Costs continue to rise, many companies have invested in equipment for compacting trash and/or baling their recyclable materials to reduce their Trash Hauling Expenses. As a distributor of such equipment, Resource Equipment Company designs, installs and services Waste Recycling Systems for large and small operations.
Resource Equipment Company is an authorized factory representative for Max-Pak and International Balers (IBC), Galbreath Trash Compactors (Wastequip), Karl Schmidt & R.E.M. Conveyors, Processing Equipment (glass breakers, magnetic separators, can flatteners and can blowers) and Shredders (Vecoplan) of all types.
Resource Equipment provides sales and service for waste recycling systems.
