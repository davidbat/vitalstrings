Process of transforming garbage into electricity?
Answer:
There are several. Two of the most popular are:
One process uses the methane produced from the anaeobc decomposition of organic material either in landfills or in digestors to run a gas powered turbine. ALternately the methane can be used directly for building heat or in fuel cells
Another processes incinerates the combustibles at high temp to heat water for steam turbines.
