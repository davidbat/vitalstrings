Organics Processing - 2012 Request for Proposals (RFP)
The City of Seattle is requesting proposals in 2012 for processing organic materials produced by City residential, commercial and self-haul customers. Proposals will be accepted for processing all or a portion of the City’s approximately 90,000 tons of yard debris, food scraps, food-soiled paper, compostable bags and packaging collected by the City’s residential and commercial contract services as well as from self-haul customers at City transfer stations. The City may award one or multiple processing contracts. New processing services would begin in 2014 or 2015.
