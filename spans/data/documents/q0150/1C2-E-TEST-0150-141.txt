 
2 Cubic Yard Skip – The Mini Skip
The 2 cubic yard skip, often referred to as the mini skip, is great for small clearance jobs. Mini skips can be used for the disposal of a variety of waste types, including general household waste, garden waste, building waste and recyclable materials such as wood and metals.

Capacity: 2 cubic yards (1.5 cubic metres)
Height: 2′ 6″ (0.76 metres)
Length: 4′ (1.2 metres)
