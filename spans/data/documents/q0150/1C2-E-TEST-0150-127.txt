 
Description of the Super Sack
The Super Sack is the solution to making bulk materials accessible. Capable of holding 1.0 cubic yard or 1.5  tons of material, the Super Sack brings a new level of convenience to any landscape project. Constructed of heavy polypropylene  fabric, the Super Sack is able to rest on a pallet for easy transportation to most job locations.
Features of the Super Sack
Heavy, flexible polypropylene
Flat bottom, for level resting
Reusable for many purposes
Specifications of the Super Sack
Capacity:  1.0 cubic yard or 1.5 tons
