How to use the tool?
This tool is designed to make it easy for you in converting from acre feet to cubic yards and vice versa. Here is a quick guide on how to use it:
1. Enter the value into the box. E.g. 10
2. Select acre foot option in the left box, and cubic yard in the right side.
3. Click on the convert button.
Then, you will see the result just below it: 10 acre feet = 16133.333297 cubic yards
Manual Calculation
You can convert from acre foot to cubic yard by multiplying number of acre foot with 1613.33333 (1 ac ft = 1613.33333 yd³).
Example: 10 ac ft = ? yd³
Solution: 10 x 1613.33333 = 16133.333297 yd³
