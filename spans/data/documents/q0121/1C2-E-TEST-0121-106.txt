Hotels Near the Sears Tower in Chicago, Illinois
A veiw of the Willis Tower (formerly Sears Tower) in downtown Chicago.
Chicago's Willis Tower (formerly the Sears Tower) soars above the skyline, making it one of the city's most recognizable buildings. The Willis Tower draws visitors to its observation deck on the 103rd floor, which offers views of up to 40-50 miles. In 2009, glass balconies that jut directly out from the side of the building were added, allowing guests to stand 1,353 feet above the city's sidewalks and look directly down on them. Because of its convenient downtown location, finding a hotel near the Willis Tower (Sears Tower) is not a difficult task.
