http://www.yctv.tv/chicago/ - The Sears Tower, located at 233 S. Wacker in Chicago's loop is one of the tallest buildings in the world.  The Skydeck on the 103rd floor provides an amazing view of Chicago and Lake Michigan. On clear days, you can see four states (Illinois, Michigan, Indiana, and Wisconsin). Internationally friendly, visitors can learn about Chicago's major landmarks in the language of their choice with the click of a button. You haven't seen Chicago until you've seen it from the Sears Tower Skydeck.
