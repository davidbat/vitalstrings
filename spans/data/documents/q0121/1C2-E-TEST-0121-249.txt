  Sears PurchaseProtect
Peace of mind for just pennies
Sears PurchaseProtect provides replacement coverage on products—three years on Craftsman® and Kenmore®, and two years on all other brands. If the product fails, you’ll receive a Gift Card for the amount of your original purchase or a replacement product of comparable value. Now that’s peace of mind!
It’s easy to get your product replaced
1  Keep your sales receipt and this contract in a safe place
2  If your product fails during the coverage period, call 1-800-573-1088 or use this website to register or make a claim . Don’t forget, you’ll need your original sales receipt.
3  You will be issued a Sears gift card equal to the original purchase price, so you can replace your product or select another product at Sears or on sears.com
