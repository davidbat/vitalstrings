Friday, March 11, 2011
Japanese Earthquake Maps
A massive 8.9 magnitude earthquake has hit off the north-east coast of Japan causing a tsunami that has caused extensive damage. The earthquake struck about 250 miles  from Tokyo and there have been powerful aftershocks.
Map from Earthquakes in the Last Week
Google has released a Person Finder for Japan. Using the application it is possible to search for information or add information for someone who may have been affected by the earthquake.
