he Hawaiian and Pacific Collections are internationally recognized research collections. All formats, languages, subjects, eras and levels of treatment are collected. Each Collection also includes manuscripts and archives, such as Hawaiian Sugar Planters' Association (HSPA) Plantation Archives , Dole Corporation Archives , and Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands Archives .
The Collections have their own reference service, circulation counter and hours. Many services vary from the general Library procedures -- for more information, see the Policies links below.
