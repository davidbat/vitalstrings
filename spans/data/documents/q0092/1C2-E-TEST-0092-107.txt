Hawaii Pacific University tops CMS 6-3
Claremont, CA (2/13/10) – In the second day of its mainland  road trip, the HPU men’s tennis team defeated Division III #4 Claremont  Mudd-Scripps College, 6-3. HPU improves to 3-1 on the season with the Stags  falling to 1-1. The Sea Warriors took control of the match after a clean  sweep in doubles play.
