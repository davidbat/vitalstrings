Long Description:
Hawaii Pacific University is a private, nonsectarian, coeducational institution offering a variety of bachelor's (and some master's) degrees. It has two campuses: the older is the one listed here, in downtown Honolulu. Spread along Fort Street Mall near the Cathedral of Our Lady of Peace, this campus serves the majority of the business and liberal arts needs of the student body. HPU is in NCAA Division II, and competes in the Pacific West Conference.
