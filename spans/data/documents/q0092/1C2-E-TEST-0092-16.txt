Accredited Online Masters Degree Programs
In the heart of the Pacific, Hawai‘i Pacific University is ideally located   at the crossroads of East and West. Hawai‘i Pacific University is a comprehensive, regionally   accredited university with a global perspective. Many of Hawai‘i Pacific   University’s programs, such as the Master of Arts in Organizational Change (MA/OC),   have over 60% of students completing degrees online. Master degree programs attract   students from the United States, Sweden, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Argentina, France, Japan,   and many other countries. Students find a dynamic, creative environment where online   programs incorporate theory with practice. Talented faculty bring their expertise   and professional backgrounds into the online classroom.
