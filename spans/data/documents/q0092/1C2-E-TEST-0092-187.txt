Hawai'i Pacific University
Hawai‘i Pacific University was founded in 1965 and has grown to approximately 8,000 students, including 2,000 students from more than 100 countries.
Hawai‘i Pacific University has 8,000 students from more than 100 countries and all 50 states; 21% are international and 79% U.S. students.
Accreditation: Hawai‘i Pacific University is accredited by the Accrediting Commission for Senior Colleges and Universities of the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC) and the National League for Nursing Accrediting Commission (NLNAC).
