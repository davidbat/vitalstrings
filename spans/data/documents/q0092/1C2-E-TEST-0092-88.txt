Hawai'i Pacific University offers the Master of Arts in Organizational Change, Master of Business Administration, Master of Science in Information Systems, and the Bachelor of Science in Business Administration programs completely online. These online programs replicate HPU's "traditional" on-campus classroom-based degree programs.
The graduate level Professional Certificate in Organizational Change and Development is offered in several formats. The distance learning format replicates the existing on-campus certificate program but courses are delivered primarily over the Internet.
