Ramada Maingate at the Park is located in the heart of Anaheim across the street from Disneyland  and Disney’s California Adventure Park. There are great restaurants within walking distance of the hotel. Each morning, Ramada Maingate at the Park offers its guests a complimentary continental breakfast that you  can enjoy at the pool. Feel free to relax and drink your coffee at the hotel before you head off to Disneyland.   You can avoid the lines at the Disneyland ticket booths and purchase your tickets at the front desk. Consider    the Ramada Maingate at the Park, when you select a hotel in Anaheim for your next Disneyland vacation.
