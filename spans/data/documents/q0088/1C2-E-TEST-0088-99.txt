About Disneyland
Devoted to Walt Disney's original theme park, Disneyland, in Anaheim California where it all started way back in 1955.
Photo Posting Guidelines
Post your finest images depicting what you love about Disneyland to share with Disneyland lovers of all ages. These can be pictures of Disneyland icons and landmarks or visits with friends and family recently or from your childhood. Your photos should be from anywhere in the Disneyland Resort, including Disneyland Park, Disney's California Adventure Park, the Downtown Disney District and the official hotels of Disneyland.
Note: Please post photographs from only the original Disneyland in Anaheim, California.
