
Whitney-Fan.com: Whitney Houston's Movies
Whitney Houston made a highly successful transition to feature films       with her first role in the 1992 blockbuster " The Bodyguard ."       Since then, Whitney's starring roles have earned more than a half billion dollars at the box office. In addition, Whitney is a highly       successful producer of movies including " Rodgers and Hammerstein's       Cinderella ," " The Princess Diaries " and " The Cheetah Girls " series.
The Whitney-Fan.com movie section includes articles, reviews, box office results and awards information about each of       Whitney Houston's movies , as well as additional film projects that have been at various stages of development.
