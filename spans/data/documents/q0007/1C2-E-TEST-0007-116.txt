Clive Davis has denied reports that he is working to produce a biopic about late singer Whitney Houston “as soon as possible.”
Famous female celebrities such as Rihanna, Vivica A. Fox, Jennifer Hudson, Jordin Sparks and Brandy were reportedly in the running to play the famous singer.
However, a representative for Davis has shot down the rumors.”There is no truth to this,” the spokesperson said . ”Clive is not putting together a biopic.”
