Flags fly at half-mast at all fire stations
(08-11 13:10)
The flags at all fire stations in the territory flew at half mast today in  mourning for the two firefighters who lost their lives trying to put out the blaze at a 15-story residential building in Mong Kok Sunday.
Hong Kong Fire Services Department Staffs General Association spokesman said that they will set  up a condolence account to help families of the two heroes, Siu Wing-fong and Chan Siu-lung.
The association is also communicating with frontline staff  to render them moral support.
