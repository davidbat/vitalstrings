Flags to Fly Half-Staff Sunday
ALLEN (May 15, 2011) – President Obama has issued a proclamation for the American flag to be flown at half-staff in honor of Peace Officers’ Memorial Day and Police Week on Sunday.
The lowered flags will commemorate officers lost in the line of duty from Texas and across the country.
A resolution approved in 1962 established Peace Officers’ Memorial Day as May 15 of every year and the week in which it falls as Police Week.
