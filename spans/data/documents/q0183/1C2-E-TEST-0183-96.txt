Flags Fly At Half-Mast
The flags at the Yankton County Government Center flew at half-mast Monday to honor the victims of Saturday’s shootings in Arizona. Gov. Dennis Daugaard has ordered South Dakota flags to fly at half-mast through Friday. (Kelly Hertz/P&D)
