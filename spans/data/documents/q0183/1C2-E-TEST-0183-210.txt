Flags will be lowered to half staff across the state today to honor U.S. Marine Corps Staff Sgt. David Day, the governor’s office announced today.
Day, of Gaylord , died April 24 while conducting combat operations in Badghis province, Afghanistan. The 26-year-old had been assigned to 2nd Marine Special Operations Battalion, Marine Special Operations Regiment, U.S. Marine Corps Forces Special Operations Command at Camp Lejeune, N.C.
Prior to his service in Afghanistan, Day had served two tours of duty in Iraq.
