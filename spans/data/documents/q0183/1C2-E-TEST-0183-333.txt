flag flying
For information and guidance on flag flying, please consult the College of Arms , the Crown body with responsibility for flags and other heraldic matters for England, Wales and Northern Ireland. For advice please email: enquiries@college-of-arms.gov.uk
For those in Scotland please contact the Court of the Lord Lyon at lyonoffice@scotland.gsi.gov.uk
Flying the Union Flag
UK Government Departments have the freedom to fly the Union Flag on their buildings all year round in addition to the designated fixed flag flying days .
We encourage everyone to follow this example, to champion the Union flag and fly it daily as a symbol of national pride.
