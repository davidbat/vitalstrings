US, State Flags to be Flown at Half Staff Today
NATIONWIDE - 4/19/2010
Printer friendly
This is to advise you that the United States Flag and Maryland State Flag are to be flown at half staff on Monday, April 19, 2010, from sunrise to sunset.
President Barack Obama has proclaimed April 19, 2010 as National Day of Service and Remembrance for Victims and Survivors of Terrorism, 2010.
This is in honor of the individuals who lost their lives as a result of terrorism.
