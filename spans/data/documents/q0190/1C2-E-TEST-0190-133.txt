complex daily oscillations in sea level, ranging worldwide from barely noticeable to several meters. tides flood (rise) and ebb (fall)- the daily migration of the shoreline landward and seaward causes significant changes that affect sediment erosion and transportation. causes of tide: earth's relation to the sun; tides are influenced by the size, depth and topography of ocean basins, by latitude, and by shoreline slope. spring tide- "spring forth," neap tide- "without the power of advancing"
