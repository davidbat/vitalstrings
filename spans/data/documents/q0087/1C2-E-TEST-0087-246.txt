Biography
Roozbeh Jafari is assistant professor at UT-Dallas. He received his PhD in Computer Science (UCLA) and completed a postdoctoral fellowship at UC-Berkeley. His research interest lies in the area of wearable computer design and signal processing primarily with the applications of health-care and wellness. His research has been funded by the NSF, NIH, DoD (TATRC), AFRL, AFOSR, SRC and industry (Texas Instruments, Tektronix & Telecom Italia). He is the recipient of the NSF CAREER award (2012) and the RTAS 2011 best paper award. He is the director of the ESSP Lab .
