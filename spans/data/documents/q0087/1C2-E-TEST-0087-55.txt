ComputingFest is the annual programming project competition sponsored by the Department of Computer Science at UTD, the local chapter of the ACM, and the Honors Students of the Erik Jonsson School of Engineering and Computer Science.
Students present their outstanding computer programming projects that began either as a class assignment or solely from their own creativity. For registration and more information, please follow the links on the left.
