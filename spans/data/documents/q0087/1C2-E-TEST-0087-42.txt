Welcome to the CGA Lab!
The Computer Graphics & Animation (CGA) Lab at the University of Texas at Dallas was founded in 2006, and is directed by Professor Xiaohu Guo . It consists of graduate students (Ph.D. and M.S.), undergraduate students, and post-docs. It is the place where researchers share their ideas, knowledges, and enthusiasm. Its main research agenda includes computer graphics, computer animation and simulation, geometric and physically-based modeling, computer-aided geometric design, virtual reality, scientific visualization, and medical imaging. The CGA Lab is located at EECS 4.620.
