Chaired Professors
The University of Texas at Dallas aspires to be a first-rank public research university with focused centers of excellence, prepared to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing, technology-driven global society. At the core of this goal is a talented and vibrant faculty that provides world-class educational and research opportunities to an impressive student body. Endowed and chaired professors form the backbone of the faculty at UT Dallas and provide exemplary leadership through a variety of research centers and programs that cross disciplinary lines. The chaired professors at UT Dallas, listed below, continue to frame and answer the questions faced by business, policy makers, health care, and the public in the State of Texas, the nation, and the world.
