Research Explorer offers a comprehensive browse feature to all users on and off campus that allows users to browse through paged results to view profiles and also filter results by profile type, department and name and also patent type.
The browse feature is available to users whether they are logged in or not.
To access the browse feature you can:
Type the following URL in the address bar on your browser or simply click this link: http://explorer.utdallas.edu/browseprofiles.php
From the Research Explorer home page click on the type of profile you wish to browse under the "Browse" box.
OR if you are already logged in, you can visit the browse feature at any time by:
Hovering your mouse pointer over the "Browse" menu link on the top toolbar.
From the drop down menu that opens, click on the type of profile you wish to browse.
