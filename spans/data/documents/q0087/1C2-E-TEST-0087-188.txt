About OrgSync
OrgSync is the organizational network for students to find out what’s going on and how to get involved on campus. OrgSync encourages student involvement and increases student awareness and accessibility to campus organizations and other involvement opportunities.
Students - search and join organizations, find involvement opportunities and manage your involvement and leadership experiences.
Student Leaders – manage all your organization’s activities and operations, recruit new members and build your organization website.
Campus Leaders – manage your department/program’s activities and operations, recruit students to join your portal and assess program learning outcomes.
