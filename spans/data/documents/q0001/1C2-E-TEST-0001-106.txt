Michael Jackson’s death certificate has been released.
According to the certificate, obtained by Extra TV , the King of Pop is listed as a 50-year-old divorced black male. His profession is classified a musician – a career he’s listed as having pursued for 45 years.
The singer’s older sister La Toya Jackson is listed as the “informant,” the individual who provided the information detailed on the certificate.
Michael’s death has been “deferred,” as are the results of his official autopsy pending toxicology results, which are expected later this month.
