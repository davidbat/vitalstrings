Book Description
Publication Date: October 2, 2009
Michael Jackson died with six different drugs in his body.  He used multiple drugs to treat anxiety and insomnia. Several drugs he took contributed to his cardiac arrest. Three drugs he used are treatments for alcohol withdrawal. One drug he took has been abused by hospital personnel and patients. He even recorded a song about a drug he used. Get the medical facts about how years of prescription drug abuse ended in the sudden death of Michael Jackson. Includes medical journal references.    The upcoming trial of Michael Jackson's doctor will hinge on various types of medical evidence. Those who want to understand the nature and significance of the medical evidence that emerges during the course of the trial will find that this book explains medical concepts relevant to the death of Michael Jackson using easy-to-understand, nontechnical English that can be understood by readers who do not have a scientific background.
