Michael Jackson died on June 25 , 2009 at the age of 50, after suffering cardiac arrest at his home in Los Angeles. Several media sources posted photos of the star being wheeled on a gurney and assisted by paramedics.
Later, an autopsy showed Jackson died from an overdose of an anesthetic propofol.

The Call and Hopitalization
A call to 911 was made from Jackson's home at 2:21 pm on June 25, 2009. When paramedics arrived on the scene, Jackson was unconscious and did not have a pulse. Paramedics administered CPR on the way to the hospital. According to a Los Angeles Times report, the musician never regained consciousness.
(June 25, 2009)
The actual call has been obtained by gossip site TMZ, and can be heared on their site .
Autopsy
An autopsy was performed on Jackson on June 26 , 2009. On August 24 th, it was revealed that Jackson died as a result of "lethal levels" of the anesthetic propofol.
(August 24, 2009)
Finally Laid to Rest
After several weeks of investigating the exact cause of Jackson's death, his body was entombed in a mausoleum at the Glendale Forest Lawn Memorial Park in California on September 3, 2009.
