Parks Department Annual Summer Programs
Posted on: Tuesday May 11, 2010
The City of Texarkana, Texas Parks & Recreation Department is hosting several fun and educational programs through its annual Summer Program.  Registration is currently underway for all of the following activities at the Parks Administration Office at the Southwest Center, 3222 W. 7th Street, (903) 798-3978.
Summer Camp
Summer Camp is for youth ages 6 - 14 at Beverly Community Center and Southwest Center.   The Summer Camp program will be held June 14 – July 30, 2010 (closed July 5-9) Monday through Friday from 9:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.  There is a one-time fee of $100.00.
Volley Ball Skill Camp
Volley Ball Skill Camp with Robin Holland for students in grades 4-6 will be held June 14th – July 14th on Mondays and Wednesdays from 1:00 p.m. – 3:00 p.m. at the Southwest Center.  This skills camp focuses on all aspects of the game with emphasis on the fundamentals.  The one-time fee is $30.00.

Tae-Kwon-Do
Tae-Kwon-Do classes for ages 5 and up with James Miller will be held June 14th – 18th or July 12th – 16th Monday through Friday from 10:00 a.m. – Noon at the Southwest Center.  There is a one-time fee of $40.00.  A free uniform will be provided.
Tennis Lessons and Play
Tennis lessons and play with Tennis Instructor John Coffee will be held June 14th through August 30th on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays at the Southwest Center.  Ages 5-8 will meet from 9:00 – 9:45 a.m. with a fee of $20.00 per month.  Ages 9-13 will meet from 10:00 – 11:00 a.m. with a fee of $30.00 per month.  Ages 14 and up will meet from 11:30 a.m. – 1:00 p.m. with a monthly fee of $40.00.
Spoken Word/Poetry Camp
A Spoken Word and Poetry camp will be led by Brittany Ray to allow students ages 13-18 to have an outlet to express their perspectives, emotions, and experiences.  The camp will also build language skills and self esteem.  Through the spoken word, students will be motivated to explore written and oral expression through poetry.  The camp will be held June 14th – July 8th on Wednesdays and Thursdays from 11:00 a.m. – 1:00 p.m. at the Southwest Center.  The one-time fee is $22.00.
For more information, call the Parks office at 903-798-3978.
