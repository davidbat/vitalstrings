Business Climate in the Texarkana Ark-Tex Region
The Ark-Tex Region covers nine counties in Texas and one county in Arkansas, with Paris, TX, Texarkana, TX and Texarkana, AR as major population centers. Manufacturing , distribution and agribusiness are the top industries in the Ark-Tex Region, which is a base for Sara Lee Bakery Group , Campbell Soup Company and Ocean Spray Cranberries . Quality-of-life attractions range from the historic Perot Theater to lakes and state parks.
