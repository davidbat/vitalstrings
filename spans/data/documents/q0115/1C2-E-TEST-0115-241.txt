Today, TrekNews.net received the promotional poster for the upcoming Star Trek: The Next Generation special theater events, scheduled to take place next month.
The events will promote the release of the first season of TNG on Blu-ray, which is set to hit stores on July 24th and is now available for pre-order through Amazon .
To promote the release of the set, the day before the release, Monday July 23rd, theatrical screening events will be held at approximatly 600 theaters across the US. The screenings will include the episodes “Where No One Has Gone Before” and “Datalore.”
