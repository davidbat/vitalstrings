Cats Guide Franny Syufy received an interesting question from a viewer recently: "Do cats purr when they are alone?" That simple question was the seed for an interesting article exploring both the how and the why of cat purr sounds. I have witnessed many cats purring under seemingly stressful conditions, so I appreciate Franny's mention of purring being a type of "self-soothing" or Ommmm type of sound that cats do in stressful situations to calm themselves.
