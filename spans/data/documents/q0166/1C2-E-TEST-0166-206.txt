RGS REBAR
Rebar Detailing Software
RGS REBAR is a simple, user friendly and most productive Rebar Detailing Software.            Developed to meet the challenging quality requirements of large sized projects            with frequent revisions and fast turn around time. Hundreds of projects from            different countries across North America, Europe, Middle East and Asia are            detailed using RGS REBAR. It can produce CNC Bar Lists to suit popular production            software. A Global product for International companies. It can also support multiple            detailing codes.
Preview Scrap and Offcut bar details while detailing itself. Adjust the            detailing length to produce Optimized BBS / Bar List.
