Rebar is one of the most widely used reinforcement materials in the world. Produced from molten steel and then run through a series of stands within the mill; the unique cross hatching pattern on rebar helps to transfer loads between concrete and steel. Rebar's high tensile strength combined with the compressive strength of concrete make it one of the most durable and economical reinforcement materials for construction.
