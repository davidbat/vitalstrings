
Alamillo Rebar, Inc., specializing in reinforcing steell and post-tension cables in N. California
Alamillo Rebar, Inc. is a reinforcing steel (rebar) fabricator and installer serving Northern and   Central California. We also furnish and install post-tension cables. Alamillo Rebar is located in Vallejo, California on Mare Island. 
Some projects include public and private low, mid, and high-rise concrete buildings, seismic retrofits, parking structures, and public works projects such as bridges, waste-water treatment plants, pump stations, and many other civil engineering projects.
Alamillo Rebar is a family owned business  and we are proud of our dedication  and commitment to excellence.
