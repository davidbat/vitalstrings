Rebar Rock Bolt
Characteristics
Resin grouted rebar rock bolts are used as permanent ground support in areas which require strong support. The rebar bolt is installed after the resin has been placed in the borehole and provides stable support once the resin has hardened.
 
DSI’s rebar rock bolts are available with forged head rebar and threaded rebar
Rebar rock bolts are available in different sizes to suit individual customer requirements
Rebar rock bolts can be supplied with blunt or slashed ends
DSI also supplies installation accessories such as nuts, washers, and installation tools
Equipment
For installing underground products, DSI offers comprehensive equipment such as the patented tandem anchor
As an additional service, DSI offers professional support during installation worldwide
