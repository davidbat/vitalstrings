Russell Ira Crowe is an New Zealand - Australian actor . He was born on April 7, 1964. He is most well known for the movie Gladiator . This movie gave him his fame in America . He won the Academy award (Oscar) for Best actor in 2001 , for acting in Gladiator.
Crowe had attended Auckland Grammar School before moving to Australia.
He is married to Danielle Spencer . She is an Australian signer, songwriter and actress. They have two sons, Charles Spencer Crowe (born 2003) and Tennyson Spencer Crowe (born 2006).
