Crowe, DiCaprio and Scott on "Body of Lies"
Russell Crowe was in rare form at the LA press conference for Warner Bros Pictures' "Body of Lies" starring Crowe and Leonardo DiCaprio, and directed by Ridley Scott. Crowe sat down and was ready to face the press before DiCaprio came into the room, and he couldn't resist the temptation to point out the fact he'd beaten DiCaprio to the table and was ready to take questions before his co-star.
