Source: Daily Mail
October  4, 2011
The Daily Mail has posted the first photos of Russell Crowe as Jor-El on the Man of Steel set in Vancouver.
Crowe stars opposite Henry Cavill, Amy Adams, Diane Lane, Kevin Costner, Michael Shannon, Antje Traue, Ayelet Zurer, Christopher Meloni, Harry Lennix and Laurence Fishburne in the Zack Snyder-directed Superman movie, scheduled to hit theaters on June 14, 2013.
