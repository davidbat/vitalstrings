Russell Crowe Movies List
A-list celebrity actor Russell Crowe was born 7th April 1964 to Jocelyn Yvonne and John Alexander Crowe in New New Zealand. He raised in Sydney when his family moved to Australia at the age of four. Russell's got first big break with Australian-made Romper Stomper. After that, movies like Virtuosity, L.A. Confidential Crowe made his real attention in Hollywood. But it was Gladiator (role: Maximus Decimus Meridius) that brought him a Oscar glory in 2000 and rise Russell Crowe's status as one of the truly gifted actors of his generation. He also nominated for Academy Award another 2 times for The Insider (1999) and A Beautiful Mind (2001). Other Crowe's notable films includes Master and Commander: The Far Side of the World, Cinderella Man, American Gangster, 3:10 to Yuma, State of Play. In 2010 he again teaming with Scott (director of Gladiator) acted in the historic role of Robin Hood. The following contains a list of Russell Crowe movies that he involved with.
