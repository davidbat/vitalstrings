Russell Crowe tweets his workout routine
July 11, 2011
Tweet by tweet, Russell Crowe is getting in shape.
The "Gladiator" star has been using his Twitter account to report on his summer exercise routine since June, People.com reports.
A sample tweet: "First ride in quite some time. Sore legs, sore a**, be worse tomorrow and the next day."
Crowe recently signed on to co-star in the new "Superman" movie as Clark Kent's dad.
