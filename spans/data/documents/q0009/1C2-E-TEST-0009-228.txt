How to Play ""Farewell Andromeda"" by John Denver
"Farewell Andromeda (Welcome to My Morning)" is the last song on singer-songwriter John Denver's album "Farewell Andromeda," released by RCA in 1973. Like many of Denver's songs, "Farewell Andromeda" is simple to play and easy to sing along with. The entire song consists of the same three chords strummed in the same pattern in all of its sections.
