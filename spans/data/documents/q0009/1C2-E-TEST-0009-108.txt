John Denver was born on 31 Dec 1943 in Roswell, New Mexico, U.S.. His birth name was Henry John Deutschendorf, Jr. His is also called John Denver . John Denver died on 12 Oct 1997.
John Denver was one of the most popular acoustic artists of the 1970s in terms of record sales,
recording and releasing around 300 songs, of which about 200 were composed by him.
