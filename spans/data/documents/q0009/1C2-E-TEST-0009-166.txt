Welcome to Friends of John Denver!
"If I should live for ever and all my dreams come true,
my memory of love will be of you."
Perhaps Love - John Denver
Would you like to meet others and share your appreciation of John  Denver and his work? Would you like to join us in keeping alive John's  music and his vision of a brighter future for all on Planet Earth?
Then why not join Friends of John Denver? We are based in the UK, but welcome members from anywhere – and it’s free!
We maintain an email list to keep members informed of anything that  comes our way related to John Denver, and of the regular gatherings  we hold to share memories, enjoy John's music and raise money for  causes that he supported.
For further details see our Membership page.
