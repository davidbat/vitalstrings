John Denver
John Denver was an American folk singer / songwriter and folk rock musician.
In the summer of 1997, John Denver recorded a children's train album for Sony Wonder, entitled All Aboard!, produced by long-time friend Roger Nichols. The album consisted of old-fashioned swing, big band, folk, bluegrass, and gospel styles of music woven into a theme of railroad songs. This album won John Denver a posthumous Best Musical Album For Children Grammy, which was his only Grammy.
