Morrison, Colorado (PRWEB) June 04, 2012    Steve Weisberg, former John Denver band member, is doing his part to keep the legendary name of John Denver uniquely personal with his "John Denver Off the Record" event on June 7th at the Marriott West.  Steve was hand selected in 1973 by John Denver to play lead guitar, dobro, pedal steel and to write songs for John. Steve is a key contributor on 7 of John's 9 multi-platinum albums, from the era John described as 'his biggest years'. Read More
