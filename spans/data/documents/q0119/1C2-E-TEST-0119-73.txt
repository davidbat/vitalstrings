Apple Store Workshop
Boston Society of Architect’s Public Art Initiative Committee , Artaic – Innovative Mosaic and Boston’s Apple Store are hosting a “Public Art in the Digital Age” workshop on December 3. The purpose of the workshop is to bring architects and artists together to integrate artistic mosaic installations into building practices.
Date: December 3, 2010
Time: 6 pm to 8 pm
Location: Apple Store, 815 Boylston St. Boston, MA
Twelve teams of architects and artists will collaborate at workstations set up in the Boylston Apple store – utilizing Artaic’s mosaic design software to create unique mosaic designs. These teams will be selected from the pool of registrations by a panel of jurors from the architecture, design and art fields.
