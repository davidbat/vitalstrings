Description
Find the closest DQ®. What more do you need?
Craving a Dilly® Bar in an unknown town? How about a Blizzard® Treat on the go? Chicken Strip Basket in your own backyard? We didn’t just build a sign to help you find us, we built an app. Because at DQ, Good Isn’t Good Enough!
Features
- Find a restaurant by ZIP Code, city or your current location
- View select store hours and contact info.
- Switch between Map View or List View
- Uses your iPhone’s GPS coordinates to find the closest DQ restaurants
What's New in Version 2.0
- Updated graphics
- Improved messaging for location finding
- Support for retina devices
