June is Black Music Month! Hell, we recognize BMM 14 months a year at Okayplayer, but to the rest of the world, I mean. And by rest of the world I mean Apple, as in the people that design your iEverythings. They are recognizing this special month with an epic string of live musical performances at their stores across the nation, featuring a who’s who of OKP favs from Jill Scott (tonight in Chicago) to Pharoahe Monch (tomorrow in SF) but also including Miguel, Big K.R.I.T., Ziggy Marley, Res and tabi Bonney among a whole lot of others. Find the complete listing after the jump and check the site for your local store for more details.
