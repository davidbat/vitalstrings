Tower Hill Botanic Garden
The Tower Hill Botanic Garden has several distinctive gardens, like the Lawn, Secret, Cottage and Wildlife Gardens, Harrington Preservation Apple Orchard and now, and the new Systematic Garden. The Worcester County Horticultural Society operates Tower Hill Botanic Garden as its headquarters on a 132 acre site in Boylston, Massachusetts.
