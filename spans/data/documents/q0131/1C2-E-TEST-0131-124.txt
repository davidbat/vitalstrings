Trinidad & Tobago Photos | Best Hotels in Trinidad & Tobago
Why go: The Caribbean doesn't see a lot of traffic during the summer months; travelers tend to head to the islands when winter is in full swing and when the threat of tropical storms is non-existant. This can mean great deals on both airfare and hotels during the summer. But here's the kicker: the islands outside the hurricane belt also offer summer discounts. Trinidad and Tobago is a prime example, and its best activities —including its outstanding beaches—are free for everyone to enjoy.
