Florida: ActionQuest
Sarasota, Florida - Offers multi-level sailing and scuba certification programs for  teens, ages 13 to 19, with  marine science, seamanship, windsurfing, water skiing and leadership training. Shipmates live aboard 50 foot yachts and sail throughout the British Virgin and Leeward Islands. Cultural and historical voyages also take place in the Mediterranean, Galapagos, Australia and Tahiti.
Florida - Beyond the Water for Kids Foundation
Orlando, Florida: As an alternative to the common non-profits that try to help children after they are in need or after they have begun to have problems in society, they recognize children that are at-risk and try to prevent a life down the wrong path.
