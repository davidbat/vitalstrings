California Water Towers: Cute, Quirky and Comical
Most towns and cities have boring water towers, shaped like cylinders or spheres painted blue to hopefully blend into the sky and not cause a visual intrusion. In a few places, people have another idea, making them look like coffee pots, glasses of milk and all kinds of fanciful stuff.
