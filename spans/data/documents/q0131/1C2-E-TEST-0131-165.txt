 
 
Teen travel camps visit multiple locations. You can find  residential teen summer camps outside of North America, at our sections on Summer Camps in Europe , Community Service , and Study Abroad .
Allen"s Guide divides teen travel camps into:
Teen Tours includes suitcase and hotel type teen summer travel to experience the culture and history of different places.
Adventure Travel covers backpack and tent style travel with activities such as hiking, rafting, sailing, mountaineering, and biking.
Cultural Teen Travel includes suitcase and hotel type trips where teens experience the culture of different countries and learn about their history.
