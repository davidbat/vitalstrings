Fishing and boating anyone?  Smallwood State Park is located on the Potomac River, one of the East Coast`s best locations for Bass Fishing and Tournaments occur here throughout the year.  Smallwood State Park`s 628 acre park includes a 50 slip marina, six boat launching ramps, a picnic area, three pavilions, and a recycled tire playground. Fifteen camping sites and six camper cabins are available for rent during the season.  The park also includes unique historic areas and miles of hiking trails.  Pets are welcomed in the day use area and certain parts of the camping areas.
