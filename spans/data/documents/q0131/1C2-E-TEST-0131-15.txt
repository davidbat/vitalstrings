Lair of the Golden Bear Family Camp - Pinecrest,  California
Nestled deep in the Stanislaus National Forest at Pinecrest, California, you'll  find the Lair of the Golden Bear — an unforgettable summer vacation spot owned  and operated by the Cal Alumni Association since 1949. A special  home-away-from-home for Cal alumni, families and friends, this beautiful  mountain retreat allows campers to unwind in the spectacular Sierra Nevada  Mountains without worrying about cooking, cleaning, or what to do with the kids.
