Quinoa: What You Need To Know
April 19, 2012 – 9:56 pm
Quinoa is one little versatile food. As the popularity for the grain continues to grow, it is rapidly becoming gluten free and dieting eaters’ choice of carbohydrates. If you are the few that hasn’t tried cooking with quinoa, you’ll find that it’s easy and goes with many dishes.
Here are a few things you need to know about quinoa:
Quinoa is great for gluten-free eaters.
Many think quinoa is a grain, but it is actually a seed. Quinoa in the raw (plain quinoa, not quinoa flour or cereal) is naturally gluten free and is a great alternative to many gluten side dishes, like pasta or oatmeal.
