About Suzuki
Suzuki Motor Corporation is a Japanese corporation headquartered in Hamamatsu, Japan that specializes in many different applications of internal combustion engines. They are responsible for manufacturing compact automobiles and 4x4 vehicles, a full range of motorcycles, all-terrain vehicles (ATVs), outboard marine engines, and wheelchairs. It was originally founded in 1909 by Michio Suzuki.
