Ichiro Suzuki (鈴木 一朗, Suzuki Ichirō) (born October 22 , 1973 in Toyoyama , Nishikasugai , Aichi Prefecture , Japan ) [1] , is a Japanese baseball player playing as right fielder for the Seattle Mariners .
He is the first MLB player to enter the Japanese Baseball Hall of Fame and has won a Gold Glove award in each of his first years ten years in the major leagues.
