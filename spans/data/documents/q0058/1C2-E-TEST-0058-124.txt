The JFK Assassination: Planned by LBJ?
ABOUT THE BOOK
John Fitzgerald Kennedy, or JFK, the 35th President of the United States of America, was assassinated at 12:30 pm at the Dealey Plaza, Dallas, Texas on Friday, November 22, 1963. At the time of his assassination JFK was considered to be among the most popular of all U.S. Presidents. Despite numerous reports and investigations, JFK's assassination has yet to be fully explained. There are a variety of conspiracy theories that question the motives behind the assassination
