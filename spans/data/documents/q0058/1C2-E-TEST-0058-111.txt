The National Archive has released audio recordings from the day of JFK’s assassination. It features LBJ expressing condolences to President Kennedy’s mother, and also some revelations about the Air Force Chief of Staff General Curtis LeMay. Gen. LeMay butted heads with JFK, and I guess he has been talked about in various JFK conspiracy theories. What is revealing about this tape is that Gen. LeMay’s aide was trying to get a hold of him but he was airborne at the time. I’m not sure what all of this means, but apparently it means something to some of the JFK conspiracists.
