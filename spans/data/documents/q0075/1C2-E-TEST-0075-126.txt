IT’S OFFICIAL: Mike Brown Hired As Head Coach
May 24, 2011 by lakersinsider | 0 comments
Man..I pray to god they talked to Kobe before hiring this guy.
Our sources tell us that the Lakers will in fact hire Mike Brown as their new head coach. Brown is set to sign a four-year, $18 million deal sometime early Wednesday.
Brown, a Greg Popovich protege, lead the 2007 Cleveland Cavaliers to the NBA Finals and is known as a defensive minded coach.
