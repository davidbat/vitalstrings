About Los Angeles Lakers
The Los Angeles Lakers are a National Basketball Association (NBA) team based in Los Angeles, California. They are members of the Western Conference Pacific Division along with the Golden State Warriors, Los Angeles Clippers, Phoenix Suns, and the Sacramento Kings.

About Los Angeles
Los Angeles is the most populous city in California. It is often known by its initials, LA, and is a center for entertainment, media, culture and fashion. LA is located in Southern California and is surrounded by mountains, valleys, desert and the Pacific Ocean. It may be most famous for its Hollywood district, a place where movies are made.
