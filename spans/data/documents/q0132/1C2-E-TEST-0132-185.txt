MOUNT SNOW RESORT, VT
MOUNT SNOW - The Closest Big Mountain Ski Resort in Vermont.
Ski and snowboard Vermont at Mount Snow. Featuring four mountain faces including the #1 ranked East Coast Terrain Park - Carinthia; snow tubing; ski school & snowboarding lessons; and slopeside lodging. Mount Snow is also Vermont's premier four season resort with an 18 hole golf course, wedding receptions and conference center, downhill mountain biking, music festivals, and more!
