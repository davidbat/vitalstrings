October 27-28, 2012
10am - 5pm
Join us for the 3rd Annual Great New England BBQFest featuring some of the area's best bbq vendors! 
Highlights of this year's event include the OYSTERFEST and Shucking Competition on Saturday, PEOPLE'S CHOICE AWARDS featuring Chili on Saturday and Pulled Pork on Sunday! Stay hungry on Sunday for a traditional PIG ROAST sponsored by the NE BBQ Association! 
We'll have great bands performing all weekend long. Then there's the ever-famous APPLEFEST Farmers' Market and Craft Fair throughout the weekend, a fully stocked Beer Tent, and loads of great entertainment for the whole family, including side shows, music and kids activities!
