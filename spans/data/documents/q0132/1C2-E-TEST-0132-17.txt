Northern New England Skiing
New England Ski Areas
Northern New England Skiing attracts thousands of tourists to northern New England ski resorts each season. With such great skiing and so many other possibilities for things to do it's no wonder.
Skiing in Vermont is some of the best. After all it is where skiing was started in America. So come visit this historic state and experience first hand what makes the skiing so great.
See the white mountains of New Hampshire the way they are meant to be seen. By skiing on them. With such a wide variety of terrain it is the perfect place for a group that varies in skiing levels and preferences.
Maine has several magnificent mountains to choose from. It's no wonder people come from all over to ski in Maine. Come and discover for yourself why the US Ski Team has trained in Maine as well as hosted many national championship competitions.
