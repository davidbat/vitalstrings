Cross Country Skiing in New England
Welcome to New England Cross Country Skiing, where you'll find the most comprehensive directory of Nordic ski areas for Maine, New Hampshire, Vermont, Massachusetts, Rhode Island and Connecticut.
To find a cross country ski area anywhere in New England, just click on the Connecticut, Maine, Massachusetts, New Hampshire or Vermont buttons above. That will take you to a list of the best xc ski areas, winter recreation destinations and four-season resorts for that state. For even more detail on individual Nordic ski areas and resorts, click on the name and you'll get all the information that's currently available.
Once you find the ideal place to ski,  get out and enjoy some of the best - and healthiest - skiing in the country!
