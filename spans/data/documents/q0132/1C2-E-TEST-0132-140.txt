The Balsams - Wilderness Ski Area
The Balsams - Wilderness Ski Area is located in Dixville Notch, New Hampshire  with 16 ski trails and 5 glades, served by 6 lifts. With 95 kilometers of cross-country ski trails The Balsams - Wilderness is considered one of the top cross-country ski resorts in North America.
Waterville Valley Ski Area
Waterville Valley Ski Area is located in Waterville Valley, New Hampshire, with 52 ski trails, served by 12 lifts. With a 4,000’ summit, Waterville Valley is one of the highest mountains in New England boasting a 2,020’ vertical drop. The nearby village of Waterville Valley offers a wide variety of ski and stay packages.
