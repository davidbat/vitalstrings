holiday valley
Consistently rated by visitors as the best ski resort in Western NY, Holiday Valley is a small, charming resort with great skiing. The mountains may not rival those in the west or New England, but the terrain is challenging and offers quite a bit of variety. A large number of lifts keeps people moving smoothly, and the excellent grooming keeps the trails fun even when there isn't a lot of snow. Holiday Valley is ranked 4th in eastern North America by SKI Magazine.
Holiday Valley Ski Essentials:
Slopes/Trails: 56 (31% Beginner, 37% Intermediate, 32% Advanced)
Lifts: 2 High-Speed Quad, 8 Quads, 1 Double, 1 Surface Lift, 1 Magic Carpet
Snowmaking: 95%
