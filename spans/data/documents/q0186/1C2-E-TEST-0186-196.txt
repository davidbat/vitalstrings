Scinax alcatraz
Scinax alcatraz is a species of frog in the Hylidae family. It is endemic to Brazil. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests, freshwater marshes, intermittent freshwater marches, and heavily degraded former forest. It is threatened by habitat loss.
