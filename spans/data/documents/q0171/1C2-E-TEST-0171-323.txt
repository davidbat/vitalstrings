Geouser the mini counter
Addresses IP and geographical location
GeoUser is the most discreet counter. It personalize the visitor to your site, giving his personal profile (IP address, browser, platform, a town close to the point of connection).
GeoUser available in two sizes as shown below. Thanks to its simplicity and mini size it will always find its place in your site. It is recommended, for example, in addition to a counter geomap or geoglobe .
