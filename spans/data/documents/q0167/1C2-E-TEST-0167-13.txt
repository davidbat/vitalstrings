Synopsis
An escaped mental patient enjoys his newfound freedom by stealing a pneumatic drill in this satire from Israel. He takes his find and digs from Tel Aviv to the sea, turning the city's streets into canals and causing chaos amongst its elected officials. In Hebrew with English subtitles.
