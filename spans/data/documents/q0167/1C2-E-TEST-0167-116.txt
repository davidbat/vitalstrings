 
Description 
Now you can be in charge of your own construction site right at your own workspace! Well, at least a small one... The Executive Sandbox Mini - Big Dig comes with everything you need for a pretend day at a miniature construction area. Use the construction vehicle, construction cone, boulder, gravel, shovel, and grooming rake to get your plot of land ready for future buildings. You can rake the sand, move the gravel, push the boulder and dig with the shovel according to your orders. It's about time to get ready for the big dig!
