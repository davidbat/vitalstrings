Dig Big
Game Description
Get ready to dig for treasures by playing another cool game in which you will have to find as many treasures as possible to gain a big score. Before the game starts you will be able to see the places where the treasures are hidden. Spot them exactly, memorize their places and dig them out to raise your score, before the time runs out. You get bonus time if you dig up the timer, you loose a life if you dig a bomb and you gain a life if you dig life guard. Reach the target score before the time runs out to be able to get to the next level. Enjoy!
Game Instructions / Controls
Use the mouse to click the place you want to dig.
