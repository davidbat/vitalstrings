N. Main Street Reconstruction
During 2010 - 2012 Barre's North Main Street with be reconstructed.  This project is a federal highway project with the funding being 95% Federal, 3% State, and 2% Local.  The estimated $16m - $18m project will be completed by the fall of 2012.  For further information please see www.wedigbarre.com
