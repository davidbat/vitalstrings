Knicks’ Steve Novak Breaks Out Aaron Rodgers’ Celebration [Video]
Former Marquette star and current member of the New York Knicks, Steve Novak was on fire yesterday in his team’s win over the Dallas Mavericks, burying three three-pointers in a span of 68 seconds. So what did he do after he hit the third? He whipped out Aaron Rodgers‘ title belt celebration.
“I’m a huge Packers fan, I was a huge Brett Favre fan and now obviously Aaron Rodgers,” Novak said. “Our team security had been trying to get me to do it for a long time. I didn’t even think about it, but it felt good.”
