About Joe Arroyo :
Álvaro José Arroyo González (also known as "Joe Arroyo" or "El Joe"; born 1 November 1955) is a Colombian salsa and tropical music singer and songwriter. Joe Arroyo was born in Cartagena. At the age of ten, he was already singing in one of Cartagena's popular music halls. Read more...
