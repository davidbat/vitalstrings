Cristiano Ronaldo Dos Santos Aveiro
Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on February 5th, 1985. He is a Portuguese football player who is as a winger for the famous Spanish Club Real Madrid, altough he got international fame with the English Premier League club Manchester United and the Portuguese national team.
Cristiano Ronaldo began his career as a youth player at C.D. Nacional of Portugal but his amazing soccer capabilities led him to a move to one of the Portugal’s major soccer clubs, the Sporting Lisboa.
Ronaldo’s unlimited talent got the attention of Manchester United manager Sir Alex Ferguson and he signed the 18-year-old for £12.24 million in 2003.
Ronaldo earned his first cap for the Portuguese national team around the same time.
By 2004, Ronaldo won his first club trophy with the Manchester United, the FA Cup, and reached the final of the UEFA Euro 2004 with Portugal, scoring his first international goal during the tournament.
