In: Cristiano Ronaldo
Answer:
Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese football player from Funchal, Madeira. His full name is Cristiano Ronaldo Dos Santos Aveiro.
He was named world football player in 2008 and also received many young player awards.
Cristiano was traded from Sporting Lisboa in 2003 to Manchester United and then was traded 6 years later to Real Madrid.
Ronaldo also debut on the Federacao Portuguesa de Futbol in the Euro 2004 in his home country.
In 2009 Cristiano Ronaldo became the highest payed player in football history when traded to Real Madrid for $132 million USD.
He was born in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal on February 5, 1985. He is 25 years old.
He was signed with Sporting (Portuguese team) at a very young age. He is an inspiration to thousands around the world.
