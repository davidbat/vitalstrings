06/28/2012 Busquets: Portugal Has Many Excellent Players, Not Just Cristiano Ronaldo
The Spain is going to fight against the Portugal in the Euro Cup semi-finals. Alonso says that the game will be more tough than the game against France. The integrity of the Portugal is better than the France. Alonso has very good understanding of Cristiano Ronaldo and Pepe. He says that they will fight the rival to the best of their abilities because the final is just one more step further.
Regarding the fact that the Portugal has two more resting days than the Spanish. Casillas thinks that it is not a big problem. This could not be an excuse of poor performances. We have been used to two game in a week. In order to win the title, you must defeat every rival. No matter who we are playing with, we will move forward.
