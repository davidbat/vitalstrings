Kofi Annan Speaks Firmly to Syria’s Assad Over Abuses
June 2, 2012 By Staff Leave a Comment
International envoy Kofi Annan on Saturday criticized President Bashar al-Assad for failing to comply with a peace plan to end the  Syria conflict and said his forces were carrying out atrocities, arbitrary arrests and other abuses regardless of the world outcry against him. At a meeting with members of the Arab League, Annan gave a bleak assessment of the situation in Syria 15 months on from the start of the anti-Assad uprising
