Note to Petitioners
Beginning June 1, 2009, all United States citizens accompanying immigrant visa applicants to Juarez must possess a valid U.S. passport or passport card to cross back into the United States. Visit www.travel.state.gov for a complete list of acceptable travel documents and for information on applying for a passport.
