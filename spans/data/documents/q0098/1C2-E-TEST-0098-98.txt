Renovated in 2010/2011 with a French Quarter decor, experience a rich pallet of colors and French Quarter inspired artwork. The Best Western French Quarter Hotel offers a complimentary hot breakfast served each morning for our guests before they head out to enjoy all the wonderful treasures New Orleans has to offer, such as Bourbon Street, located only three short blocks away. The Best Western French Quarter Landmark Hotel places you steps away from world-famous restaurants, boutiques, antique shops, and entertainment. When you are ready to relax, our lush tropical courtyard with a fountain and outdoor swimming pool provide a refreshing atmosphere for any traveler. Our management and staff are eager to provide courteous, professional and efficient service with a smile. We look forward to welcoming you to the Best Western French Quarter Landmark Hotel where the streets of New Orleans come to life.
