The Landmark offers more than just a great location. The hotel, with its intimate, European feeling reflects a traditional New Orleans style. Check in time is 4pm.
Two luxurious Jacuzzi suites feature antique reproduction furniture, wet bar, microwave, refrigerator, queen size sleeper sofa and king size bed.
