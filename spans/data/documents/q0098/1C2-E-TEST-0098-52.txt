Map Help:
Click on the map, keep the mouse-button pressed and now move your mouse to move the map around. This will also change the value of Latitude/Longitude of the map (note: the Lat./Long. values correspond to the map center).
Point with your mouse cursor on a desired place on the map then double-click to center the map.
To zoom in click on +, to zoom out on –
The Map Type Control Buttons
To switch between various map types use this buttons: Map for street map, Satellite for satellite image; Hybrid for a combination of satellite image and street map; Terrain for a physical map.
