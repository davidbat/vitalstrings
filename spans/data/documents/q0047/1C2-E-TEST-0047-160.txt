Pro-life; supports state restrictions on abortion
Q: What is Tom Corbett's view on abortion?
A: Tom Corbett is pro-life and believes in the sanctity of human life. In 2005, Tom Corbett stood with other Attorneys General in supporting New Hampshire's abortion control statute before the US Supreme Court, which had been challenged by Planned Parenthood. Tom's action ultimately protected Pennsylvania's Abortion Control Act by helping to preserve restrictions on abortion nationwide. He received endorsements from the Pennsylvania Pro-Life Federation.
Source: Campaign website, tomcorbettforgovernor.com, "FAQ" , Nov 2, 2010
