Executive summary: Governor of Pennsylvania
Military service: Pennsylvania Nat'l. Guard (1971-73 and 1975-84, Capt.); Texas Air Nat'l. Guard (1973-75)
Former state Attorney General elected Governor of Pennsylvania in 2010, succeeding Ed Rendell . Conservative Republican; generally anti-abortion rights, anti-gay rights, pro-gun rights, anti-medical marijuana. Campaigned promising smaller, more efficient government, lower taxes, reduced regulations, and deep budget cuts.
Father: Thomas W. Corbett
Wife: Susan Manbeck (one son, one daughter)
Son: Thomas, III
