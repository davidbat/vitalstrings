What Makes Leaves Green?
Glad you asked...
Chlorophyll, a green pigment used in the photosynthesis process, is what makes plants green. More accurately, what makes plants green is chlorophyll's poor ability to absorb green light.

Leaves of a plant are green because of chlorophyll. Chlorophyll is found in the chloroplasts of a plant cell. Chlorophyll is green in color and is used to absorb energy from the sun.
