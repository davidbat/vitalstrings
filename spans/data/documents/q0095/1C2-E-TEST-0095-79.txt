American Airlines Arena
Best known as the home of the NBA's Miami HEAT, the American Airlines Arena Miami also hosts concerts and other special events. The 20,000-seat AmericanAirlines Arena debuted with Gloria Estefan's Millennium Concert Spectacular on New Year's Eve, 1999. Since then, the auditorium in downtown Miami has been the stage for some of the biggest stars in the entertainment world, among them the Rolling Stones, Luciano Pavarotti, Bette Midler, Gloria Estefan, Julio Iglesias, Elton John, Liza Minnelli, U2 and Britney Spears. The American Airlines Arena Miami has also been the scene of the MTV Music Video Awards, the Latin Grammys, The Greatest Show on Earth and other spectacles. The AmericanAirlines Arena was once the home court for the Miami Sol, the now-defunct WNBA team.
