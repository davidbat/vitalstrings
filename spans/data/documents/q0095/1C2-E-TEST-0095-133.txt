NBA games and much more
American Airlines Arena is the home of the NBA'S Miami Heat franchise, as well as one of the finest concert venues in the country. Ice shows to unlimited excursions at clubs and shops highlight this colossal 20,000 seat venue on Biscayne Bay.
Restaurants, bars, retail shops, and on-site covered parking are just of few of the things offered. Check the stadium's website or call for a list of scheduled events during your stay in Miami, you may even catch the circus. Just the view of the bay is actually worth the visit.
