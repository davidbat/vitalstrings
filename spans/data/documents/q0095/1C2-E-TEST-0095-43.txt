American Airlines Arena
United States
The American Airlines Arena, located in downtown Miami, is home to the NBA's Miami Heat. The arena seats up to 20,000 people for sporting and entertainment events. It includes 2,105 club seats, 80 luxury seats and 76 private boxes. The Waterfront Theater housed within the American Airlines Arena can seat up to 5,000 people for concerts, family events and stage productions. Kirlin's work included all plumbing and mechanical work within the arena.
