Facts
The arena occupies 8.4 acres on Biscayne Bay.
The arena seats 19,364 for basketball games, 17,000 for hockey, and 5,000 to 15,000 for concerts.
The arena contains twenty "super suites" that feature balconies overlooking Biscayne Bay.
A large airplane and the American Airlines logo is painted on the arena's metal roof.
Over 1,000 parking spaces are located below the arena.
