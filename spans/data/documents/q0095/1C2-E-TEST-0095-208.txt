How Does GottaPark Work?
Search
Search for parking around your destination. Compare price, distance and descriptions to       find the right spot for you.
Reserve and Print
Reserve your selected spot using our secure payment system. Then  print out your receipt and take it with you to your destination.
Park
Your guaranteed spot will be waiting for you when you arrive. You can arrive and leave anytime during your reservation. Just show your receipt to the parking attendant. It's that easy!
