World Trade Center
n
(Placename) a former building complex, at 417 m (1368 ft) the tallest in the US, that stood in Manhattan, New York, from 1974 until its destruction in the terrorist attack of September 11 2001, in which some 2800 people were killed Abbreviation WTC
