Tony Parker
BIO
Born in 1982, Anthony "Tony" Parker was in the French basketball league before entering the NBA Draft in 2001.
Currently, Parker is a point guard on the San Antonio Spurs, helping the Spurs win three NBA Championships in 2003, 2005 and 2007. Parker has been selected as an NBA All-Star three times, an All-NBA Third Team member once, and was the 2007 NBA Finals MVP.
Parker married actress Eva Longoria, known for her role as Gabby Solis on ABC's Desperate Housewives, in a lavish Parisian ceremony on July 7, 2007; three years later, Longoria filed for divorce amidst cheating rumors, citing irreconcilable differences.
