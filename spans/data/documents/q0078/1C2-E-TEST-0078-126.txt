San Antonio Spurs point guard Tony Parker will visit McHi from noon to 4 p.m. Sunday, Oct. 16, for a basketball skills clinic.
The clinic is open to boys and girls ages 8 to 17, and pre registration costs $125. You can register by calling (956) 630-0082. Each participant receives a free shirt, an autograph and a photo with Parker.
Tony Parker Skills Clinic
WHEN: 12 p.m. to 4 p.m. Sunday, Oct. 16
WHERE: McHi, 2021 La Vista Ave., McAllen
COST: $125 in advance or $150 the day of
