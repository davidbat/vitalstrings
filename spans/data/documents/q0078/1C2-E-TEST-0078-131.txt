Has his look-alike puppet in the French show "Les guignols de l'info" (1988).
Born in Belgium, raised in France
Fluent in English and French.
Drafted by the San Antonio Spurs in 2001.
Played in the NBA All-Star Game in 2006.
Born to Tony Parker, Sr., an African-American former basketball professional, and Pamela Firestone, a Dutch former model, now working as health-food coach.
Has two younger brothers, T.J. and Pierre.
Three-time NBA Champion with the San Antonio Spurs (2003, 2005, 2007).
His favorite music styles are rap and hip-hop.
Good friends with former teammate Boris Diaw and soccer player Thierry Henry .
Married Eva Longoria in a civil ceremony, held by Paris mayor 'Bertrand Delanoë', on 6 July 2007 and in a religious ceremony at the Eglise Saint-Germain-l'Auxerrois in Paris one day later.
Is a French citizen. He plays in the National French basket-ball team.
Thierry Henry served as a witness as Parker's wedding to Eva Longoria .
