Welcome to New Life Churches International.
We are a family of contemporary churches passionate to see the Gospel of the Kingdom impact every ethnic group in every nation.

We desire to see every individual reach their full potential in life, enthusiastically participating in their local church, creatively partnering to add value to their community, actively engaged in international mission.
