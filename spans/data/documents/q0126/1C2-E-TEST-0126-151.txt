Editors Note
Y.M.C.A: The Belapur branch of the YMCA has accommodation for boys, as well as working men, with 120 beds. Theres also a well-maintained swimming pool, basketball court, gym, badminton, table tennis facilities, carom, chess, and rollerblading arena. The YMCA holds karate classes here. They have one conference room, with a capacity of holding 50 people. They also conduct outstation camps in liaison with their branch in Poona. Concessional rates are offered to churches.
