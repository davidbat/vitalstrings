Hillary Diane Rodham was born on October 26, 1947 in Chicago. She was raised in a Methodist family with her two younger brothers. As a child, Hillary was involved in Church               activities, and was a Brownie and Girl Scout. In 1965, Hillary attended Wellesley                      College. She became active in politics and while at college, joined the Democrat                      Party. In 1969, Hillary entered Yale Law School. At Yale, Hillary worked on children's issues and provided legal advice to the under-privilege. Bill and Hillary were married on Oct 11, 1975 in Arkansas. Chelsea, their only child, was born on February 27, 1980.
