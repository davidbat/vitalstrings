Rare photo of Lady Bird Johnson with Hillary Clinton.
 
In a rare photo of the two first ladies together, former first lady, Lady Bird Johnson, joins First Lady Hillary Clinton to show off the new First Ladies T-Shirt designed in cooperation with the Hillary Clinton Quarterly to honor all first ladies. The photo was taken at the White House when Hillary was First Lady.
In the enlarged version below, readers can see the complete names of all first ladies, starting with Martha Dandridge Custis Washington.
