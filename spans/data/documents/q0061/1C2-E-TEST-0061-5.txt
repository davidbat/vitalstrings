Welcome to the Web Site of First Lady Hillary Rodham Clinton. As First   Lady, Mrs. Clinton has worked to improve the lives of people here and around   the world. She has had the privilege of traveling to many different places both   within the United States and around the world to promote opportunity and   democracy. And she has made a special effort to ensure the White House, The   Peoples House, will remain a monument of our cultural heritage as the   center of our nations government.
Please use the links on this site to: learn more about the First Lady   and her efforts on issues critical to children and families; get a flavor   for some of the events Mrs. Clinton has hosted and attended; and go inside the   White House to find out more about its history and the special events   that take place here.
