By Mo Curls
The rumors are true. Dr. Dre has a new single “Under Pressure” featuring Jay-Z. The good doctor was in Fenway Park for the opening day game between the Yankees and the Red Sox (Red Sox won 9-7). He was interviewed by NESN and said that if he could choose a song to play for introductions it would be his own “Under Pressure” single with Jay-Z.
