Dr. Dre & Snoop Dogg To Headline Coachella 2012
by Steven J. Horowitz
Tweet
Additional headliners include The Black Keys, Radiohead, Bon Iver and Girl Talk.
Goldenvoice has announced the lineup for the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival, taking place in Indio, California on April 13-15 and 20-21.
Dr. Dre and Snoop Dogg are scheduled to headline on either April 15th or 22nd. Additional performers at the fest include Frank Ocean , Kendrick Lamar ,  The Weeknd , Azealia Banks , araabMUZIK , ASAP Rocky , Flying Lotus , Childish Gambino , Santigold , Company Flow and a slew of others.
