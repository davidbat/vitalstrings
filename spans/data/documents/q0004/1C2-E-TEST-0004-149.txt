Dr. Dre Says Long-Awaited "Detox" Is Coming Soon
July 24, 2008 - Dr. Dre 's third solo album, "Detox," may be released before the end of the year--nine years after the release of his most recent album, the six-times platinum "2001." Dre, whose real name is Andre Young," told the USA Today he is shooting for a November or December release, though no specific date has been set.
New Dr. Dre Book To Be Released This Week
March 24, 2008 - A new book about Dr. Dre , "Rollin With Dre: The Unauthorized Account: An Insider's Tale of the Rise, Fall and Rebirth of West Coast Hip Hop," is scheduled to hit stores tomorrow (3/25), according to Random House books.
