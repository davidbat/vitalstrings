Dr. Dre will make his first television appearance in ten years when he performs with Eminem at the Grammy Awards on Sunday!
Dr. Dre and Eminem, who has been nominated for ten awards this year, will perform together at Sunday’s Grammy Awards show.
Dre, whose real name is Andre Young, hasn’t released an album since 1999 but is nominated for Album of the Year for producing Eminem’s latest album “Recovery”.
The Grammy’s will feature performances from Lady Gaga, Christina Aguilera, Jennifer Hudson, Florence Welch, Gwyneth Paltrow, and many more.
It will be interesting to watch Eminem and Dr. Dre’s duet!
