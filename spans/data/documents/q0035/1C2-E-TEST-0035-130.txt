This remarkable company is energized around the idea that theatre positively provokes a community into thinking about both local and global issues. The work may be provocative, humorous or tragic. It will always be excellent.
2012 / 2013 Touring Season:
Tartuffe
Moliére’s classic adapted by David Ball and directed by John Kellam ably showcases the Commedia roots of The Actors’ Gang in this ageless tale of envy and deception.
1984
Directed by Tim Robbins and based on Orwell’s epic novel, this adaptation by Michael Gene Sullivan is touring the world with engagements at major festivals in Athens, Hong Kong and Melbourne.
