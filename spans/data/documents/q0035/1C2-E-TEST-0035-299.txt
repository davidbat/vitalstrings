Cape Artists Agency is one of Cape Town's longest established actors’ agencies. Our objective is to manage the careers of our established actors and to develop up and coming younger talent.  Cape Artists Agency is small by design and particularly selective with the talent we choose to represent.  Our goal is to manage the careers of each of the actors we represent; so that we might maximise their potential.  As members of the personal managers association we ensure that we adhere to an ethical code of conduct within the film and entertainment industry in South Africa.
Our clients have included Walt Disney, Paramount Pictures, Amblin & many other household names. Our actors include film and theatre award winners and It is with confidence that we present them to you.
