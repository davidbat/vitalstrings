Most of the old SCTV cast had Canadian and/or Chicago roots.
Eugene Levy - Hamilton, Ontario, Canada
Joe Flaherty was born in Pittsburgh, but moved to Chicago to join Second City Theater for 7 years, then moved to Toronto to establish Second City there. He was one of the original writer/performers on SCTV.
Dave Thomas was born in St. Catharines, Ontario
Catherine O'Hara was born in Toronto, Ontario.
Martin Short was born in Hamilton, Ontario.
Rick Moranis and John Candy have already been mentioned.
