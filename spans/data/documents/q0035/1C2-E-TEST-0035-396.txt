Founded in 1937 as the Italian Actors Union
An affiliate of                                              the AFL-CIO
A Branch of the Associated Actors and Artistes of America
The Italian Actors Union through the years                                              has experienced many transitions as other theatrical unions and guilds emerged and jurisdictions were formed. The IAU is now                                              recognized as GIAA: Guild of Italian American Actors.
GIAA is committed to helping Advance, Promote, Foster and Protect the welfare of                                              its members not only within its own jurisdiction but with the jurisdictions of its sister unions.
GIAA serves                                              the purpose of preserving the history and awareness of Italian Heritage amongst its members.
