The Actor & Actresses portal provides information about more than 10,000 famous actors and actresses.  Users may browse the complete list in order of popularity, or they may filter the list by name, nationality, or award winners.  Each actor or actress is represented by a small photo and short description, which link to a more detailed page about the individual.  Each detail page includes quick biographical facts, image galleries, and lists of movie and television appearances that star has made.  Other available information may include places in which the individual has lived, famous friends, frequently asked questions, films or shows produced or directed, awards won, related news stories, and more.
