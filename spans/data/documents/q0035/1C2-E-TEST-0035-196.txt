Actors’ Equity Association
Audition Hotline: 212.869.1242
Actors’ Equity Association is the labor union representing American actors and stage managers working in the professional theatre. Equity negotiates minimum wages and working conditions, administers contracts, and enforces the provisions of their various agreements with theatrical employers across the country. Additional bene?ts of membership include: audition notices, health and pension plans, disability insurance, agent regulations, and tax assistance. For membership information and procedures see the Equity website.
Actors' Equity provides Casting Call, a nationwide listing of audition notices for performing artists and stage managers, both online and through audition hotlines. Online users can search for regional auditions based on call type or contract.
