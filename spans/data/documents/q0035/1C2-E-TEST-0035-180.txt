Laura Marion, Actor, Singer, Artist
 Put her in front of a camera... give her a microphone... just see what she can do...
 
Laura Marion is a highly trained singer, actor, not to mention she's a professional portrait painter and graphic designer. After performing extensively throughout the San Francisco Bay Area for the first part of her life, she's lived in LA for six years and is working her tush off to build a career in television, film, and stage. She is passionate, talented, professional, super smart, and loves what she does!
