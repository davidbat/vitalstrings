Main article: Three tramps The three tramps are three men photographed by several Dallas-area newspapers under police escort near the Texas School Book Depository shortly after the assassination. Since the mid-1960s, various allegations have been made about the identities of the men and their involvement in a conspiracy to kill Kennedy. Records released by the Dallas Police Department in 1989 identified the men Gus Abrams, Harold Doyle, and John Gedney. Allegations of other conspirators E. Howard Hunt The theory that former CIA agent and Watergate burglar, E. Howard Hunt , was a participant in the assassination of Kennedy garnered much publicity from 1978 to 2000. Separately, he denied complicity in the murder of JFK while accusing others of being involved.Others have suggested that Hunt was one of the men known as the three tramps who were arrested and then quickly released shortly after the assassination.
