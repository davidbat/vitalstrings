Release Jean Dujardin and Bérénice Bejo at the 2011 Cannes Film Festival The film premiered on 15 May in competition at the 2011 Cannes Film Festival . It was initially announced as an out of competition entry, but was moved to the competition a week before the festival opened. The French regular release was on 12 October 2011 through Warner Bros. France . The Weinstein Company bought the distribution rights for the United States and Australia and Entertainment Film Distributors bought the UK distribution. The film was initially given limited release in the United States on 25 November 2011. Following its wins at the 69th Golden Globe Awards , it was announced Warner Bros. would re-release the film in France in 362 theaters on 25 January 2012. It was also re-released in Belgium on 22 February 2012.
