(December 2009) A cubic yard is an Imperial / U.S. customary (non- SI non- metric ) unit of volume , used in the United States , Canada, and the UK. It is defined as the volume of a cube with sides of 1 yard (3 feet , 36 inches , 0.9144 metres ) in length .
