"Whirlpool" (Appliances) Lowe's Build and Grow clinics Lowe's Build and Grow clinics are free workshops for children put on by Lowe's on the second and fourth Saturday of each month. These workshops are free of charge and allow children and their parents an opportunity to experience the joy of building things hands on. Children have a new project at each clinic. They are given an apron and a patch for each project completed. Projects include building boxes, napkin holders, toys such as a dinosaur, and holiday-themed items.
