Keanu Charles Reeves (born September 2, 1964) is a Lebanese-born Canadian-American-British actor best known for his portrayals of Ted Logan in Bill & Ted's Excellent Adventure and Bill & Ted's Bogus Journey, Officer Jack Traven in Speed, Neo in the action film trilogy The Matrix and Johnny Utah in the surf drama Point Break opposite Patrick Swayze .
