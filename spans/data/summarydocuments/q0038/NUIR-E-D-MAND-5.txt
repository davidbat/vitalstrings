Cameron next began the sequel to Alien , the 1979 film by Ridley Scott . Cameron named the sequel Aliens, and again cast Sigourney Weaver in the iconic role of Ellen Ripley . According to Cameron, the crew on Aliens was hostile to him, regarding him as a poor substitute for Ridley Scott. Cameron sought to show them The Terminator but the majority of the crew refused to watch it and remained skeptical of his direction throughout production.
