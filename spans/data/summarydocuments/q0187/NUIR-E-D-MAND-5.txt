The village lies on the Bromley to Westerham road ( A233 ), adjacent to the north-western perimeter of Biggin Hill Airport . It is some 5 miles (8.0 km) south of Bromley and 2 miles (3.2 km) north of Biggin Hill . Leaves Green village has a large (and eponymous ) village green which straddles the A233. This is the location for one of London's coal tax posts (number 186) which formerly denoted the boundary of the area in which the City of London Corporation was empowered to collect duty on coal . The post is also depicted on the village sign .
