Over a year after the disastrous 9.0 magnitude earthquake in Japan in March 2011, the nation continues to face the aftermath of the quake and ensuing 40 meter tsunami, which took nearly 20,000 lives and threatened many more with concerns over radiation from the damaged Fukushima Nuclear Power Plant.
