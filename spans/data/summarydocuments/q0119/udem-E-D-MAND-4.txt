A preview of the new Apple(R) Retail Store on Boylston Street for accredited media When: Wednesday, May 14, 2008 at 11:00 a.m. EDT Where: Apple Store(R) Boylston Street 815 Boylston St. Boston, MA 02116 Accredited journalists may confirm their attendance by calling (408) 974-2046. In a city famous for higher learning, the Apple Store, Boylston Street dedicates an entire floor to personal training, including Pro Labs, which offers free tutoring on Apple's pro applications. Grand Opening The Apple Store Boylston Street will open to the public on Thursday, May 15, 2008 at 6:00 p.m. EDT. From west to east, Boston's Boylston Street begins at the intersection of Park Dr. and Brookline Ave. as a two way, four lane road in Boston's Fenway neighborhood where it runs through a swath of parking lots and other underutilized space before forming the northern boundary of the Back Bay Fens at the Storrow Drive/ Commonwealth Avenue right-of-way. Location: P O Box 598, 11 French Drive, Boylston
